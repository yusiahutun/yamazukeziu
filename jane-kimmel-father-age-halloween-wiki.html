Jane Kimmel Wins Hearts Of Many Like Her Father Jimmy Kimmel

Blessed with the best, Jane Kimmel has the best daddy in the world. Jane is the youngest daughter of comedian and TV host, Jimmy Kimmel and her second wife. And, here's what you need to know about the little one.

Jane Kimmel - Daughter Of Jimmy Kimmel's Current Age Is 6

As if Jimmy Kimmel wasn't already blessed with two children, 2014 surprised the TV host with one more, and she was none other than Jane Kimmel. Well, Jane is the first child and daughter of the Jimmy Kimmel Live! host and her second wife, Molly McNearney.

Jane Kimmel, the sweet daughter of Jimmy Kimmel. SOURCE: People

To add more, Jane was born in July 2014 just a year after her parents' holy matrimony. As of 2020, Jane's age is currently six years old. As for Jane's parents, Jimmy and Molly walked down the aisle in July 2013, following their engagement in August 2012.

Not to mention, Jane's parents, in fact, worked together for Jimmy Kimmel Live! and have been together since October 2009. As for Molly, she worked as one of the head writers on the show.

Father Jimmy is also fond of showcasing his beloved daughter on his show. Matter of fact, Jane has been shown on Jimmy Kimmel Live! on the yearly traditional episodes of Halloween Candy where Kimmel pretends he ate all the candies of his kids.

And, since Jane Kimmel who completed her pre-school in June 2020 isn't the only child of her parents, she also has a younger brother and two more half-siblings from her father's former marriage.
