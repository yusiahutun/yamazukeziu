Tina Knowles' wedding look revealed

On Sunday, Tina Knowles married her long-term love Richard Lawson, and now Tina's famous daughter, pop star Beyoncé, has shared the first pictures of Tina's stunning wedding gown. The long, slinky white gown was a Romona Kevez design, which wrapped over one shoulder and had a knee-length split - perfect for a wedding which took place on a luxury yacht.

Reportedly all 300 guests wore white for the ceremony, and the groom didn't disappoint either, wearing a smart all-white suit, with white bow tie and white boat shoes.

Richard Lawson and Tina Knowles look content after their wedding ceremony

The picture, which was posted on Beyoncé's website, shows the happy couple celebrating their day with a glass of champagne on board the sun-drenched boat, which was 140-foot long and moored outside of Newport Beach.

Beyoncé, her husband Jay Z and their three-year-old daughter Blue Ivy were among the invitees, as was the singer’s younger sister Solange Knowles.

It's the second marriage for fashion designer Tina, who filed for divorce from Beyoncé's father and former manager Mathew Knowles in 2009 after more than 30 years of marriage. In October, Tina opened up about finding love again after her divorce. "I'm at my happiest I've been in a long, long time," she admitted.

Beyoncé has a very close relationship with her mother Tina

Richard, best known for his work on the soap All My Children, as well as the 1982 horror film Poltergeist, has also been married once before. The couple started dating in 2013.

Tina's wedding comes just five months after her youngest daughter Solange tied the knot in New Orleans, and where the bridal party all also wore shades of white. In November, Solange married music manager Alan Ferguson at the Holy Trinity Church in front of around 200 guests, including mum Tina, big sister Beyoncé and Jay.
