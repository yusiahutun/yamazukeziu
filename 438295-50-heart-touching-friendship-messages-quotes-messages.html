80+ heart touching friendship messages, quotes, and texts

True friends are always there with you in every aspect of life. They give you a helping hand as you face life's challenges. A good friend shares your problems and makes you laugh when you get sad. One way to cultivate such a wonderful relationship is to send heart-touching friendship messages from the heart to express how much you love and value them.

Friendship messages, quotes, and texts

Your friendship messages sound better when you add your favourite memories of you and your friend. You can customize some friendship messages from this article to suit your friend, the occasion, and your intention.

New friendship messages

Treat your new and old friends differently because each is unique in many ways. If you need fresh thoughts to write on a friendship birthday card or any other occasion, you can find a suitable sweet message for a best friend from this list:

  • I love you for being a great listener, and you can do anything for the people you care about.
  • I only want to let you know that you have the best laugh and your strength inspires me.
  • You'll always be one of my very favourite people. You're not one to sugarcoat things.
  • It means so much to know you're on my side. We love to hate all the same things!
  • I'm grateful for you because you have a refreshing inappropriate streak.
  • You're the one I can talk to when I don't have anything nice to say.
  • I'm so lucky to have a friend like you. I was afraid of trying new things and didn't want to give you a chance, but you crept into my heart so subtly that I couldn't resist being your friend.
  • Many reasons make me glad that we're friends. To name one, you're the kindest person I know.
  • Thank you for all the times you've been there for me. I thought I had all there was to friendship, but you came with another dimension, and I'm overwhelmed.
  • Thanks for putting up with me. Where were you all this while? I cherish you, and I cherish our friendship.
  • We're kindred spirits. I would have little friendship with what I knew and experienced. But now, with you, I know there is more to it. Welcome to my life. I am hoping to have you forever there.
  • Your friendship means so much. How would I have known there was more to friendship if you hadn't come around?
  • If I were to judge you by the cover, I wouldn't have you anywhere around me, but when you came close, I realized it's the hard shell covering a lot of sweetness inside. We know each other so well and make a great team.
  • The first day I saw you, I knew we were perfect together. You are a blend of everything I want. We just clicked right from the start because we complemented each other.
  • We're surprisingly alike deep down where it counts. We can be real, have so much fun, and understand each other.d each other.

Emotional friendship messages

  • Thank you so much for touching my life in ways I could never explain. You have taught me how to love and how to appreciate the life we all have.
  • You are my blessing from above, the only one who understands me in the most incredible ways. Thank you for being the best thing that has happened to me lately.
