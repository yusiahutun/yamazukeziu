Diane von Furstenberg Opens Accessories-Only Boutique

Media Platforms Design Team

A nice long walk on the Highline feels very New York and a prime aerial view can inspire many emotions, including a desire to shop. Diane von Furstenberg is adding to the options with The Accessories Shop, located in the gallery space of the DVF headquarters on West 14th Street, and housing the brand's growing collection of accessories. Launching with the Pre-Fall line, including the 440 Top Handle Satchel, mini bags like the quilted Milo and the 440 Mini and necessities with whimsy like iPhone cases, wallets and wristlets, it's all of DVF's extras in one spot.

The Accessories Shop

440 West 14th Street

Open Mon-Sat, 11AM – 7PM/ Sun, 12-6PM

In addition, Kerry edits the “best of” each season, from shoes to bags, denim and coats, and is consistently looking for shifts in the fashion realm to lend a voice. Kerry formerly served as the Editorial Director of  StyleCaster.com and as a producer at Full Frontal Fashion. She lives in New York with her husband Steve, and baby girl Lila Sky.
