After We Fell soundtrack: List of songs from the 2021 movie

Great music and a great film go together. After We Fell is a 2021 film with a good storyline and a moving soundtrack. If you watched it, you might wonder about the song choice. What songs feature in the movie? And what is the After We Fell soundtrack?

If you are a sucker for a romantic drama, then After We Fell is the movie for you. The flick has all you can ask from a romantic film; enthralling, tearjerking and romantic.
