Carianne Marshall Named COO At Warner/Chappell

published on March 23, 2024
LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — Veteran label exec Carianne Marshall has been named as Chief Operating Officer at Warner/Chappell Music. Marshall will be based in Warner/ Chappell’s Los Angeles offices starting in June, and she will report to Warner/Chappell Chairman & CEO Jon Platt. Prior to joining Warner Music’s publishing unit, Marshall most recently was a partner and Head of Creative Services, and Head of Creative Licensing at SONGS, the prominent indie music publisher, where she worked with artists such as Lorde, Diplo, DJ Mustard and The Weeknd among others.
