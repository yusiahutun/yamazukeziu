Who is replacing Chanel on Ridiculousness? All the details

Ridiculousness, an American comedy clip show, started airing on August 29, 2011, on MTV. The show is hosted by Rob Dyrdek and co-hosted by Sterling "Steelo" Brim and formerly Chanel West Coast, who is now leaving after 12 years. But don’t worry. Chanel West Coast's absence will not be all gloomy as the show's executives have announced an impressive all-star lineup of replacements. And they have already started. So, who is replacing Chanel on Ridiculousness?

Chanel West Coast left Ridiculousness as a co-host and is stepping into the role of an executive producer. But fans shouldn't be worried at all. The show's executives have an exciting lineup of co-hosts as replacements. So, who are these famous personalities?

Why is Chanel leaving Ridiculousness?

Chanel West Coast announced the sad news of her departure from the popular television show amid mixed reactions from fans. And according to her departure statement, her primary reason for leaving is her next big venture with MTV/Paramount, where she will take the role of an executive producer.

Who is replacing Chanel of Ridiculousness?

MTV went all-in to find a suitable replacement for Chanel West Coast, whose co-hosting skills were incredible. And they came up with several renowned personalities to fill her place in Ridiculousness. The A-listers started making appearances on April 3, 2023. So, who hosts Ridiculousness now?

1. Brie "Bella" Garcia

Briann Monique Danielson, known professionally as Brie Garcia, is an American TV personality and retired professional wrestler. She is renowned for her time in the WWE from 2007-2023. Brie retired from the WWE in 2023 after her contract expired.

2. Brittany Elena

Brittany Elena is another popular personality in the all-star lineup replacing Chanel West Coast. She is a social media influencer, model, actress, TV host, Instagram star, and YouTuber. Brittany Elena rose to fame when she appeared in Nick Canon’s Wild ‘N Out.

3. Camille Kostek

Camille Kostek is a model, TV presenter, and actress. She has impressive hosting skills considering she has been the on-field host of the game show Wipeout on TBS. Camille has also hosted NBC’s Dancing With Myself since 2022

4. Carly Aquilino

Carly is an American stand-up comedian, television host, and actress. She was a regular cast member of MTV's Girl Code comedy television series. Carly was also a host of Girl Code Live. With years of experience in television and as a host, Carly is an excellent co-host for Ridiculousness.

5. Draya Michele

Draya Michele is a famous actress, model, fashion designer and American media personality. She is renowned for her role in Alaska, We Belong Together, and Be Someone (2023). Draya is an entrepreneur and owns various fashion lines. She launched another clothing line in November 2016 called Beige & Coco.

Another fabulous co-host that will be taking over after Chanel West Coast is Karrueche Tran. She is a renowned actress and model who has starred in various notable productions. In 2022, she hosted Upcycle Nation, a Fuse TV competition show on sustainable fashion.

7. Lolo Wood

Lauren Wood, popularly known as Lolo, is a model, fitness trainer, and actress. She is also known for being the boyfriend of Odell Beckham Jr. and appearing on Nick Canon’s TV show, Wild N Out. 8. Maddy Smith
