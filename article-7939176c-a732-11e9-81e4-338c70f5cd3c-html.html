South Jones senior competing for DYW title | Education

Anna Donald of Ellisville is one of 34 Mississippi high school seniors who will be competing for more than $28,900 in college scholarships when the 2019 Distinguished Young Women of Mississippi Program gets under way Thursday in Meridian. 

The daughter of Chad and Brooke Donald, she attends South Jones High School. She is part of the Future Leaders of Jones County, Beta Club, Key Club and is Duke TIP Recipient. She plans to attend University of Southern Mississippi to pursue a career as a forensic scientist.
