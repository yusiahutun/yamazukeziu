Jennifer Hudson Debuts Super Short New 'Do -- Like the Look?!

Not everyone can pull off a super short hairstyle, but Jennifer Hudson is looking fierce!

The "American Idol" alum took to Instagram to debut her mane makeover after she traded in her longer pixie cut for a nearly-bald 'do.

“All black every thang! Who needs hair, when u serving face!” she wrote with the sexy selfie.

The singer is no stranger to switching up her look, playing around with the length of her hair over the years.

When she first chopped off her tresses last year, she admitted that the cut made her feel like a whole new woman.

"The hair change is almost as drastic as my weight loss! It feels the exact same way," she told PEOPLE. "Shorter hair makes me look taller, so I'm still trying to figure that out … If you are gonna wear a different style, you have to wear it. You can't let it wear you."

Not only did she chop off her hair, but she also added some bling to her ears. "I just got my ears pierced at the top too. It's more about the profile than anything for me and elongating myself even more. I'm trying to find things to complement that. For me, short hair is all about presence. You have to have presence."

J. Hud definitely has a presence!
