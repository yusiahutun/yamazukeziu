4 of Asias richest celebrity power couples: from Bollywoods Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh

When an actor reaches a certain level of fame, their whole life becomes a movie – with fans as fascinated with their off-screen antics as their next screen role. And when two celebrities hook up, the effect is simply multiplied.

So as the leading superstars of Asia’s film industry, these celebrity power couples are as well loved for their great performances as their on- and off-screen chemistry. We’re always excited to see these celebrities together, whether it’s at an event, commercial campaign or in a film.

After staying together for many years, not only are these couples some of the happiest in the entertainment industry, they’re also known for being the region’s highest-paid actors with multimillion dollar fortunes and lavish lifestyles.

So who makes our list?

4 uber-rich Hong Kong icons who came from humble beginnings

1. Hong Kong’s Tony Leung and Carina Lau

Veteran actors Tony Leung, 59, and Carina Lau, 56, are one of the most talked-about couples in Hong Kong thanks to their successful careers and long-lasting relationship – of 33 years. After dating for 19 years, they got married in 2008 and are still seemingly just as happy together.

According to Today, Leung admitted in an interview that his wife was his “remedy” and he appreciates their “comfortable” relationship. In a separate interview, Lau revealed that their relationship “isn’t one where we’re in the best state of love. But I can say that we’re both in the best condition, and we’ve also found a way to communicate with each other after being together for so long. We have a comfortable relationship and it works for us.”

Not only are Leung and Lau the biggest stars in Hong Kong’s showbiz scene, they are also savvy business investors, growing their purse year by year. Lau is the owner of her own fashion brand Anirac and some restaurants and bars in Hong Kong, Beijing and Shanghai. Her fortune is estimated at US$61 million. Meanwhile, her husband, recently star of Marvel’s Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings, also earns millions from property investment, alongside his acting and singing pay, which allowed him to build up a net worth of US$20 million, per Celebrity Net Worth.

The pair reportedly live in a luxurious US$13 million house and enjoy a high-class lifestyle of travelling by private jet or yacht, collecting antiques, sailing and surfing.

2. South Korea’s Rain and Kim Tae-hee

Hallyu star Rain (Jeong Ji-hoon) and actress Kim Tae-hee are known as K-biz’s golden couple because of their endearingly sweet relationship. The adorable couple are often seen openly flirting with each other whenever they get the chance. According to Kbizoom, when Kim finally uploaded old photos of herself on Instagram after seven months, Rain left a comment on the post, calling his wife “gorgeous” with a lot of heart emojis.

Kim once sent a coffee truck to her husband at the set of Ghost Doctor, with a cute teasing banner that said: “He is very smart, but if you know him well, he’s really weird.”

In addition to their huge achievements in the entertainment world, boasting some top-paid paid acting gigs and lucrative endorsements, both of them are known as real estate moguls. While Rain and Kim’s net worths are rated at US$55 million and US$16 million, respectively, per Kpop Starz and Celebrity Net worth, they reportedly also own a combined US$72 million worth of properties, according to 8 Days. These include the shared US$4.4 million house in Seoul and another US$2 million in California, while Kim owns three Gangnam buildings and Rain has his hands full with a villa and shopping centre in Cheongdam.

3. India’s Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh

Stars Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh were recently voted as the most powerful couple in Bollywood, according to a survey by the Indian Institute of Human Brands. The “DeepVeer” brand, as fans call them, is favoured for being the most fun loving, charming and different, collecting 86 per cent of the votes, beating the famous runner-up couple Anushka Sharma and Virat Kohli.Former model Padukone is one of Bollywood’s highest-paid actresses, earning some US$10 million per year from films and endorsements, with a total fortune of US$40 million according to Celebrity Net Worth. She also collaborated with Indian fashion e-commerce company Myntra to launch her own clothing line, All About You, and is a writer for women’s health and fitness magazines.

Meanwhile, her husband Singh has also built most of his US$20 million net worth from acting and commercials with top names like Adidas, Kellogg’s, Durex and Head & Shoulders, also according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Last year, the couple bought a US$2.6 million bungalow in Alibaug, on an area of around 18,000 sq ft and only 10 minutes from the beach, becoming neighbours with Bollywood royal Shah Rukh Khan, NDTV reports.


4. Singapore’s Fann Wong and Christopher Lee

Fann Wong and Christopher Lee were once called “Singapore’s Angelina Jolie and Brad Pitt” when their Hollywood counterparts were still together, a sign of their influence on the Lion City. Now together for two decades since 2002, including a wedding in 2009 and a son, their relationship is said to be “forever”. Lee once wrote in an Instagram post on their wedding anniversary: “Happy anniversary, my baby. How many years have we been together? There’s no need to count, because it is forever.” Awwww.

Successful not only in modelling and singing, Wong also shines on screen, winning many acting awards and became the first Singaporean actress to play a major role in a Hollywood film, Shanghai Knight. As among top highest-paid actresses in Singapore, she has a net worth of US$28.7 million, per Newshub360.

Like wife, like husband. According to Movies Trix, Malaysia-born Lee also made history for his acting career as the first non-Singaporean to win the local Star Award for Top 10 Most Popular Male Artistes. At the end of last year, he turned his golf passion into business, launching a golf clothing brand, LMS. It is expected to help increase his estimated net worth of up to US$5 million.
