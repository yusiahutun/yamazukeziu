Jennifer Lopez Proves the Perfect Accent to a Sweatsuit Is a Luxe Fur Coat

Time and time again, with each dance and workout session, Jennifer Lopez proves athleisure can be utterly fabulous—you just need a couple of glamorous accents.

The queen of high-low fashion put her maximalist style on display once again, arriving at a dance studio in Los Angeles on Friday. The base of Lopez’s outfit included an oversize slate-gray zip-up hoodie layered over a simple white T-shirt, paired with matching sweatpants and pale pink Allbirds Tree Flyer 2 sneakers. She then zhuzhed up the sporty ensemble in the most J.Lo way possible—with a large, fluffy beige fur coat that featured streaks of caramel brown throughout. But she didn’t stop there; Lopez pulled an icy-white Birkin accented with gold hardware from her enviable Birkin collection, and added oversize aviator shades and chunky gold hoop earrings. She kept her makeup bronzy and pulled her hair back into a neat topknot.

Bellocqimages/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

This is just the latest of luxe-casual outings for the multi-hyphenate. Last Tuesday, she returned to her dance studio wearing a black turtleneck and matching yoga pants, layered underneath a long beige coat and paired with white sneakers, the same chunky hoops, and large black sunglasses. That time, she was armed with a glossy chocolate-brown crocodile-embossed Birkin.

Earlier this month, the “On the Floor” hitmaker was photographed with her 16-year-old twins, Max and Emme, seemingly coming or going from rehearsals. For the occasion, she sported another gray sweatsuit, this time composed of baggy low-waisted sweatpants and a cropped crewneck sweater with billowing sleeves that showed off her killer abs. She finished the look off with short platform Ugg boots in chestnut, rectangular brown shades, and, of course, a rare matte cream Niloticus Crocodile Birkin.
