Desperate Housewives Had Some of the Best Guest Stars Ever See Our Favorites!

Let’s be real, a Desperate Housewives reboot would be the best thing to ever happen to TV. And, if all the show’s most popular guest stars could return with the iconic main cast for a revival, nothing would be better! Throughout the series’ 180-episode run, popular actors including Carol Burnett, Doris Roberts, Lily Tomlin, and more totally stole the show on Desperate Housewives. Seriously, we would give anything to see them back on the small screen alongside Eva Longoria, Felicity Huffman, Marcia Cross, Teri Hatcher, and Nicollette Sheridan.

Carol and Marcia on Desperate Housewives.

Lucky for Desperate Housewies fans, one of the show’s stars, Eva, recently dished on the possibility of a reboot — and admitted she would love to reprise her role as Gabrielle Solis! When asked in a recent interview what it would take for her to return to Wisteria Lane, the actress candidly replied, “Oh, nothing. It would take nothing! I would jump at the chance to play Gaby Solis again. I miss her! I miss her skin and I miss being in her skin. The minute [show creator] Marc Cherry says, ‘We’re going back,’ I would be the first one to sign up. I love that show and I love the magic that we had.” We would love that too, Eva!
