Amy Adams wears Gucci in American Hustle

What do you dress a sexy femme fatale in? Gucci, of course!

The Italian fashion house was called upon to accessorize Amy Adams’ character Sydney Prosser in American Hustle, a cunning and glamorous conwoman and the sidekick to infamous conman Irving Rosenfeld, played by Christian Bale.

"When I dressed Amy Adams for American Hustle, Gucci was a natural choice,” explains Costume designer Michael Wilkinson. “What better label to capture the sophistication and glamour of New York in the late 1970s?”

“Her horsebit jewellery immediately shows the audience that Amy's character is both elegant and sexy. Her Gucci platform loafers encapsulate classic style. When the director asked for a ‘special handbag’ for a detailed close-up, I was drawn straight away to the Python Bamboo Lady Lock – bold, luxurious and iconic."

American Hustle is set for nationwide release on 1 January 2014.
