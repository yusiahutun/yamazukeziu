Meet Singer Firerose, Billy Ray Cyrus Fiance! Her Bio, Age

All Figured Out singer Firerose might have stepped up her relationship with Billy Ray Cyrus. Flaunting an engagement ring in a recent IG post, the singer hinted that she could be getting married soon.

Get to know her better as this short bio on Firerose proceeds.

Meet Singer Firerose, Billy Ray Cyrus Alleged Fiance

Firerose first met Billy Ray Cyrus in 2012 on the set of Hannah Montana. Falling in love with her music Billy then stayed in touch with her ever since.

Fast-forward to July 2021, the duo acquainted again to release a song together titled New Day. The song was written over Zoom during the pandemic and is about new beginnings — a song of hope for a new day for anyone searching, reaching, and praying for a new day.

So, it was only thereafter, they continued to grow closer.

Then followed a series of IG posts featuring each other with sweet captions like “Birds of a feather” and more.

Finally, in August 2022, the lovebirds then seemingly confirmed their romance when Firerose paid tribute to Billy on his birthday. “The world is a better place with you in it. Happy Birthday, Billy,” she wrote.

Just a month later, Firerose again dropped another bomb posting an IG photo wearing a diamond on her ring finger. “Taking in the moment…” she captioned the photo, tagging Billy as the person who captured the image.

Obviously, fans then congratulated her believing she was engaged to Billy.

But Firerose herself didn’t comment on the engagement speculation, and neither did Billy who had recently split from his ex-wife and mother to his children, Tish Cyrus.

For those who don’t know, Billy and his 6 years younger wife Tish, married in 1993 following a whirlwind romance. After 29 years of age of marriage Tish then filed for divorce citing “irreconcilable differences” as the reason, also claiming that the two haven’t lived together since February 2020.

Billy and Tish share two kids named Braison and Noah (27 and 22 years of age in 2022). Also, Billy adopted Tish’s two kids Miley and Brandy (29 and 24) from her previous relationship.

Singer Firerose Career

Just like Billy, Firerose is a country singer who grew up in a musical family. They say she was writing songs as soon as she could speak.

“I honestly can’t tell you where it came from but my spirit and my soul connected to the world through music and helped me make sense of everything. I would get home from school and write a song about my day and thought that it was a normal thing to do,” the singer explained.

So, realizing this gift, Firerose cultivated it over time, whilst also teaching herself the piano. Yes, she taught herself the key. “It just felt right to sit at the keys. I didn’t know the names of the chords but I felt it and I played by ear,” Firerose recalled.

After graduating high school, Firerose then moved to LA and began performing regularly at The Viper Room, The Rainbow Room, and SXSW, garnering herself a lot of passionate fans.

She then started writing more and more songs and as of now, her 5 singles “No Holding Fire, Way Out, Fragile Handling, New Day, and All Figured Out” are loved by many. Infact, her latest single (released May 6, 2020)  “All Figured Out” has over 1.5M views on Youtube.

Talking about her music, Firerose shared that her writing was inspired by Sia, Alanis Morisette, and Australian rock bands like Powderfinger, INXS, and Silverchair. “It comes from the deepest and truest place in me. I’ve learned from country music that it’s all about authenticity and truth,” she explained.

Also, she added that music was the only thing that allowed her to express herself in a world where “you can’t just walk around expressing your innermost truth.”
