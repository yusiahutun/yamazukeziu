Kericho County is one of the counties in the Rift Valley region of Kenya. It is home to several sub-counties, each with unique characteristics and communities. So, how many Kericho sub-counties make up the entire county? What are the wards and population sizes of each? Kericho County is one of 47 counties of Kenya's devolved government. The county borders Baringo County to the North East, Uasin Gishu County to the North West, Nakuru County to the East, Nandi County to the Northwest, Bomet County to the South, Nyamira County to the West, and Kisumu County to the Northwest.
