Franklin Barnes story and latest updates: Everything you should know

The events of 29th March 2018 sent shockwaves through the peaceful community of Plano, Texas, leaving a trail of questions and shattered lives in their wake. At the centre of the tragic occurrences is Franklin Barnes III. Now, his name has become synonymous with a disturbing shooting spree that claimed lives and left a community reeling.

Franklin Lee Barnes III is a 23-year-old murder suspect from Plano, Texas. He rose to fame for being the main suspect of aggravated murder charges in a spree shooting that took place in Plano and Arlington. The events left the community grappling with confusion and grief.

Who is Franklin Barnes?

He is an American nationality known for being the main suspect in aggravated murder charges in Plano and Arlington. He was born in 2000. There are no details about his parents, siblings, early childhood, and educational background. His real name is Franklin Lee Barnes III.

Franklin's murder and killings

In March 2018, it was alleged that Franklin shot and killed two men in Arlington. He then drove to Plano and shot four more people, killing one on 29th March. Winston Davis and Joshua Daniels, both 25 at the time, were found shot dead in a car parked at an Arlington apartment complex.

Apart from the Arlington shooting, it is alleged that the two victims were robbed, too. A victim's family member said:

When they killed him, they took all his jewellery off him. They took all his rings off his hands and took all his jewellery off. They also took Winston's shoes off his feet.

Later, during the same day, another person, Quintarius Young, 29 at the time, was murdered in a shooting at Cross Creek Apartment in Plano. It is alleged that in the Plano apartment complex attacks, Young and his partner were Franklin's target.

After attacking the two victims, he randomly shot two others, making the total number of victims six. Franklin's victims were shot in the face. In addition, there was a higher likelihood that the Arlington victims were targeted because Franklin knew Winston Davis and Joshua Daniels.

Arlington's ongoing investigations

After the dreadful attacks, Franklin was arrested in McKinney a few hours later. Even though the investigation is largely focused on the Plano attacks, the police are working to establish the connection between Franklin and the two murders in an Arlington apartment complex.

When the victim's family were shown Franklin's mugshot, they said they had never seen him before. However, Arlington police officers confirmed that evidence at their crime scene was similar to evidence collected at the Plano shooting scene.

Franklin Barnes' side of the story

In the middle of all the accusations and evidence presented, Franklin maintains his innocence. His version of the story brings out a different picture, hinting at a more complex and disturbing story. In his narration, Franklin stated that he and his girlfriend were driving from Arlington.

Later, he purchased more from his friend Quintarius Young. After buying the psychoactive drug, he spent some time with his girlfriend before going back to his McKinney home. He fell asleep and didn't wake up until police officers showed up at his home Thursday morning.

Franklin Barnes now

As the legal proceedings unfold and the investigations widen, the public's question remains: where is Franklin Barnes now? As of 2023, Franklin remains confined within the walls of the Collin County Jail.

His future hangs in the balance with a bail set of $1.25 million. In March 2023, Change Organisation created a petition aiming to give Franklin a second chance, alleging he was wrongly convicted and he neither went to trial nor given the chance to give his side of the story:

I truly believe everyone deserves a second chance in life. He is now 23 and has spent five years rethinking everything that happened that day. We all make mistakes, but that doesn't define who we are. I ask all of you to join us in the petition.
