10 best football movies on Netflix that you should watch in 2023

Many say baseball is America's pastime, but football is its obsession and passion. Since time immemorial, the National Football League has dominated the airwaves throughout the season. However, watching the best football movies on Netflix is an excellent thing to tide fans over in between the heated season.

The best football movies on Netflix come in different themes and storylines. They could be a gritty portrayal of the brutal sport, an emotional tear-jerker, or a side-splitting comedy. However, the only thing that makes great football movies outstanding is that they have a heart.

Football movies on Netflix

The best football movies carry the same message to their fans. If you work hard, give everything you have, and believe in yourself, you can make it in life. Here’s a look at the top 10 best football movies on Netflix that you should watch in 2023.
