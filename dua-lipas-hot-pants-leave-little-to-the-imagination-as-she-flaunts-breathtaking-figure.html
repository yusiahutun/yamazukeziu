Dua Lipa's hot pants leave little to the imagination as she flaunts 'breathtaking' figure

Dua Lipa has stunned fans once again in another sun-kissed snap, this time, in figure-hugging hot pants. The snap comes after she wraps up her birthday week celebrations with a huge milestone.

The music star has been killing the Instagram game lately, and when fans thought she couldn’t get any more ‘perfect’, she only went and did exactly that. We take a look at the singer’s latest post, which leaves little to the imagination.

Dua Lipa shows off her “sun bum”

Just days after showcasing her curves in sheer underwear, the star is now showing off her beauty once again, as she stuns in the sun, in a pair of hot pants.

Taking to her 89 million Instagram followers, Dua Lipa posed in front of palm trees with her sand mini-shorts, as she simply captioned the post: “sun bum.”

The star’s back was facing the camera, for fans to see her curves, although she turned her sun-kissed face towards the lens, with her long black locks trailing behind.

Lipa’s ‘7’ tattoo was also on display as she bared her arms in a cream tank top.

The star has been ‘giving’ on the gram lately, as she sent fans into a frenzy as she gave off ‘real life mermaid’ vibes in a red sheer dress during her vacation.

Fans are in awe at the star’s ‘breathtaking’ beauty

As with every post, the singer’s fans are taking to the comments to compliment the star on her face, outfit, and body.

“My heart stopped for a bit,” commented one.

“Your summer outfits are everything,” penned another follower.

“Hotter than hell,” said another, referencing her hit song.

Another simply exclaimed: “SERVING!”

Dua Lipa has a great end to her birthday week

It’s smiles all around for the One Kiss singer, as earlier this week she topped off her birthday week with another huge achievement.

The star’s hit single, Dance The Night, featured in the Barbie film went to Number 1 in the Official UK Charts.

To celebrate, the newly-turned 28-year-old took to X, formerly Twitter, to share a number of snaps of herself posing with the plaque, alongside a figure of her very own Mermaid Barbie.

Dance The Night became the star’s fourth number 1 in the Official UK Charts, with her most recent being Cold Heart with Elton John in 2021.
