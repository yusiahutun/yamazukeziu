Kayden Gaulden: Meet Rapper NBA YoungBoy's First Son

Kayden Gaulden is famous as the first child and son of renowned American rapper NBA YoungBoy. Kentrell DeSean Gaulden, famously known as NBA YoungBoy, rose to fame after releasing eight independent mixtapes between 2015 and 2017. Afterward, YoungBoy got signed to Atlantic Records and released the single “Outside Today,” which peaked at number 31 on Billboard Hot 100 chart. 

The song became the lead single for his debut studio album, “Until Death Call My Name,” which he released in 2018. The album peaked at number 7 on the US Billboard 200. However, his second studio album, AI YoungBoy 2, debuted at number 1 on the US Billboard 200. Hence, it became one of the top ten biggest streaming debuts in 2019. 

Since he rose to prominence, NBA YoungBoy has achieved several career milestones and is one of the most successful rappers. However, he has faced several legal issues and baby mama scandals. Aside from his thriving career and legal dramas, the Baton Rouge-born rapper is also a father. Consequently, his fame shone a light on his eldest son, Kayden Gaulden.

Here’s everything we know about him.

Who Is NBA YoungBoy’s Son?

Kayden Gaulden was born to his parents, NBA YoungBoy and Nisha Keller, a magazine model, in Los Angeles, United States. He is of African-American ethnicity. Kayden’s birth was controversial, and it had the internet agog for days. While his father was 16, his mother was 14 when they had him, which did not sit right with most fans. 

Although Kayden Gaulden has shared his father’s fame since birth, there are no details regarding his personal life and education. However, online reports suggest he attends a prestigious elementary school in Los Angeles. While his parents shield him from the media, Kayden often accompanies his father to his shows and appears on the rapper’s social media. In addition, Kayden Gaulden appeared in YoungBoy’s music video, “Kacey Talk,” in 2020. 

How Old Is Kayden Gaulden?

NBA YoungBoy’s first son was born to his parents on July 4, 2016. Hence, he is a 7-year-old kid. Despite being in his formative years, Kayden Gaulden has embraced the spotlight. He mostly lives with his famous father and seldom stays with his mother. In addition, he also enjoys the company of his siblings. 

Does Kayden Gaulden Have a Social Media Presence?

No, he doesn’t. Kayden is a kid, so he does not have a social media account on any platform. However, he appears on his father’s Instagram story frequently. 

How Many Siblings Does NBA YoungBoy’s Son, Kayden Gaulden, Have?

Although just seven, Kayden Gaulden has ten siblings from his father’s multiple relationships. While YoungBoy is known for consistently dropping songs, he is also famous for the frequency with which he welcomes children with different women. Consequently, fans of the rapper took to Twitter to joke about how the world will consist of his descendants in the coming years.

Kayden’s siblings are:

Taylin Gaulden

Taylin Gaulden was born to the rapper and Nia on March 19, 2017. He is Kayden’s half-brother. According to reports, Nia got pregnant with Taylin after a one-night stand with YoungBoy. Hence, after Taylin’s birth, the rapper had to conduct a paternity test. 

Kamiri Gaulden

Kamiri was born on July 6, 2017, to the rapper and one of his ex-girlfriends, Starr Dejanee. Like Taylin, he is six years old. Despite being a celebrity kid, there are no details regarding his personal life. 

Kamron Gaulden

Kamron was born on July 19, 2016. Although he is not NBA YoungBoy’s biological son, he is Kamiri’s brother and the son of Starr Dejanee. YoungBoy named Kamron one of his sons. However, a DNA test later confirmed that he was not the biological father. Regardless, the rapper has been raising Kamron as his son. 

Kacey Gaulden

Kacey is NBA YoungBoy’s fourth son and Kayden Gaulden’s third half-brother. He was born to the rapper and his ex-girlfriend Jania Bania, a social media influencer and model, on February 13, 2019. 

Armani Gaulden, Kayden Gaulden’s Full Sibling

Armani Gaulden is YoungBoy’s first daughter and fifth child. She was born to the rapper and Nisha Keller in 2020. Like Kayden Gaulden, details about Armani are kept private. 

Kodi Capri Gaulden

Kodi was born on November 26, 2020. She is the rapper’s second daughter and the product of his former relationship with Drea Symone. In addition, she is Kayden Gaulden’s half-sister. 

Kentell Gaulden Jr.

Kentell Jr. is the rapper’s seventh child, born on January 9, 2021. YoungBoy had him with his then-girlfriend, Iyanna Mayweather. Iyanna is a famous social media personality. However, she is best known as the daughter of Floyd Mayweather, the famous American boxer. 

Alice Nora Gaulden

Alice is the third daughter and eighth child of NBA YoungBoy. The famous rapper had her with his long-time girlfriend-turned-wife, Jazlyn Michelle Hayes. She was born in 2021, but her exact birthdate is unknown. 

Klemenza Tru Gaulden

Klemenza was born in September 2022 to YoungBoy and his wife, Jazlyn. The rapper announced his birth through a social media post on September 28, 2022. However, he didn’t disclose his exact birthdate. While Klemenza is Kayden Gaulden’s half-brother, he is Alice’s full sibling. 

Last but not least is the recent addition to the family. He was born in April 2023 to Kayden Gaulden’s father and Instagram model, Drew Valentina. However, his name is unknown.
