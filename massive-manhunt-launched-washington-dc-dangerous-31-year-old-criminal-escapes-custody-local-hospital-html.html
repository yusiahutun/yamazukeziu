Massive manhunt is launched in Washington DC after 'dangerous' 31-year-old criminal escapes custody

A murder suspect has fled from a hospital in Washington DC and a large-scale police investigation has been launched. 

Christopher Hayes, 31, escaped from custody at George Washington Hospital in the 900 block of 23rd street at around 3.30pm on Wednesday. 

He has been described as a 6ft tall black male with shoulder-length dreadlocks and was wearing a black t-shirt, grey shorts and a white body suit. 

Hayes, who was arrested and charged with homicide on the day he escaped, is currently not in handcuffs and the public has been warned not to engage with him.

A helicopter was seen circling the area and large sections near the hospital have been cordoned off.
