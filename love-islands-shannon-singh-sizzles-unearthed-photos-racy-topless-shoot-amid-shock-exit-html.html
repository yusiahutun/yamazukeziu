Speaking after being given the boot, Shannon revealed she would be up for making a comeback. 

'Yeah, why not? It was very short lived. Surreal, but I’m obviously very grateful I got the opportunity. I’m not coming away fuming from it, I’m coming away grateful,' she said. 

'I am a bit gutted. Do you know what, out of all the girls, I think it is fair it’s me because I didn’t have any emotional attachments to the guys, but all the girls had tiny little things [going on].'

Shannon admit that she was so caught up in the drama of Chloe stealing one of the guys, that she 'forgot' re-couplings would put islanders at risk of being dumped. 

'I knew they were going to throw something in there, they always do. I knew it was going to be like she [Chloe] was going to take one of the guys. I just can’t believe I forgot there were dumpings,' she confessed. 

'I think all of us forgot there were dumpings. None of us really spoke about it.

'When I read it out Liberty was almost crying, she was like, "What?" I did get a little bit emotional and then I eased up.'

Speaking immediately after her exit on the show, Shannon had said: 'It’s emotional because you meet people straight away and you bond. I was me in there, so it is what it is. No regrets.' 

While fans have everything crossed that Shannon will get to return to the villa, she is now focusing on making more content for her OnlyFans account.
