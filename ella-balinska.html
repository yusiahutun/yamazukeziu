Ella Balinska Bio, Age, Height, Boyfriend, Net Worth, Wiki

Ella Balinska is a young and beautiful British actress. She is popularly known to the world for her role in the action-comedy movie Charlie’s Angels, which was released in 2019. Ella is still an uprising actress who has not won many awards but is expected to win many after her further future career in acting. She played one of the protagonists in one of the most popular Netflix series at the moment Resident Evil season 1.

Early Life

Ella Balinska was born on October 4 in 1996. She is the daughter of Kaz Balinski- Jundzill who was an entrepreneur and her mother Lorraine Pascale was a chef. Ella is not known to have any siblings and was raised in London, England, and belongs to a mixed British, Polish, and Caribbean background. As a student, Ella attended James Allen’s Girls School located in Dulwich, London leaving the school in 2015, after which she went to Guildford School of Acting from where she graduated in July 2018 majoring in acting.

Ella Balinska Career

Ella made her career debut by playing the role of Tanya Manson in the movie Junction 9 in the year 2015. She played the role of Clarice Spinoza in Thanatos the same year after which she appeared as Agent A. Fox in the 2016 movie Hunted. In 2017, she appeared in two short films Maya and Meghan in Clover and Room, then Cristina in 10 Men & Gwen, and then played the role of Esme in A Modern Tale released in 2017. She made her television series debut in 2018, where she appeared as Susie Barbour in season 32 and episode 33 of the series Casualty and as Grace Briggs in the Episode: Till Death Do Us Apart in the series Midsomer Murders.

In 2019, she appeared as a cameo in the short film Ru’s Angels, as Jane Kano played the same role in the movie Charlie’s Angels. She then appeared in the television series Nyela Malik in The Athens and then was seen playing the role of Cherie in the 2020 movie Run Sweetheart Run. Recently, it was announced that Ella will be seen playing the role of Frey Holland in the 2022 video game titled Forspoken.
