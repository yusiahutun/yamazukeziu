Soso, Mississippi | Local News

By J.R. Gavin

The town of Soso is less than 15 minutes from Laurel, Ellisville and Taylorsville. Its town center is located at the junction of state highways 533, 28, and 29. The Ridge Road (Highway 29) between Soso and Ellisville is the most scenic in the county.
