Cindy Costner is an American Social Activist, Model, and Actress. Cindy Costner is popular for being the ex-wife of the American actor, Kevin Costner. Her most famous acting role is in “Dances with Wolves”.

Early Life and Childhood

Cindy Costner was born on October 29, 1956, in California, USA. She is 65 years old and is under the astrological sign, Scorpio. She holds American nationality and her birth name is Cynthia Silva. She is of Portuguese ancestry.

Her father’s name is Louis Silva and her mother’s name is Patricia Joan Antonucci. She also has a sister and grew up alongside her and her name is Cathy Silva. Other than this, no further information is available regarding her other family members such as her cousins, grandparents, and relatives.

Cindy Costner – Education

Regarding Costner’s educational background and qualifications, she attended a private high school in her locality. She then enrolled in California State University and graduated with a degree in Biological Science.

Career and Professional Life

Cindy Costner was able to develop a remarkable interest in the performing arts while she was studying in high school. Occasionally, she would appear in stage plays coordinated by her school.

And, after graduating from university, she was able to land some roles in Disneyland plays. These plays included Cinderella and Snow White. She also performed in various theatres.

Likewise, she got a job as a marketing agent for the world-famous Delta Airlines after her short journey at Disneyland. However, this marketing job was short-lived. It came to an end when she was able to land her first film role in the year 1990.

It was the time when she became a part of the cast for the film “Dances with Wolves”. The movie is an adaptation of a Michael Blake novel of the same title and her role in the film was that of a wagon master. She also starred in “LiteWeight” in the year 1998.

Moreover, Costner now works as a full-time social activist. And, a lot of her works revolve around championing women’s and children’s rights and speaking out against any form of exploitation. She is also an entrepreneur and businesswoman. She owns “Palms Restaurant” and is a manager at Pasadena restaurant founded by her ex-husband.

Relationship Status

Cindy Costner tied the knot with Kevin Costner on February 11, 1978. They met each other for the first time when they were students at California State University.

They quickly became friends and then began dating. They were in a relationship for two years before getting married. Kevin, on the other hand, is a world-famous actor popular for his roles in films and TV shows such as Let Him Go, Draft Day, 3 Days to Kill, and Mr Brooks.

However, after 16 years of marriage, the couple decided to go their separate ways and eventually filed for divorce. The divorce settlement saw her walk away with $50 million.

The duo has three children together and their names are Annie, Lily McCall, and Joseph Tedrick. After the divorce, she got together with Larry and became Cindy Antonucci-Ameen.
