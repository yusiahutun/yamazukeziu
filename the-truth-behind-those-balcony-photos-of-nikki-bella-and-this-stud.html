The Truth Behind Those Balcony Photos of Nikki Bella and This Stud

Nikki Bella opens up about the younger man she was seen flirting with in Miami following her split from John Cena.

Nikki Bella is setting the record straight about the younger man paparazzi caught her canoodling on the balcony of her Fontainebleau Miami Beach suite back in May.

In a sneak peak of tonight's episode of "Total Divas," the WWE star reveals the moment was completely set up for the purpose of duping the paps -- and every media outlet that subsequently reported she was in a new relationship following her split from John Cena.

"The paparazzi are hounding us everywhere we are going. I'm not gonna lie, I don't know how to handle it," Nikki confesses on tonight's episode. "I'm in Miami for Nia and Nattie's birthday. I wanna relax and do it privately. I guess I didn't think people cared that much."

Nikki then decides to give the paps something juicy to photograph by recruiting a hot, young bartender to flirt with her on the balcony of their suite.

"Brandon, come talk to me real close," she says as the rest of the ladies watch in enjoyment. "Let's see the lies."
