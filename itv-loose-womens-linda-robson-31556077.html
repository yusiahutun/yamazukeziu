ITV Loose Women's Linda Robson makes 'swinger' confession after split from husband

Loose Women turned into carnage on Wednesday (29 November) when Linda Robson opened up about a "swinging mishap" with her husband Mark Dunford.

The 65-year-old told her co-stars during a chat about holiday chums that she'd met a lovely couple on a trip once. She explained they'd got on so well that Linda handed out her number - much to her husbands dismay at the time.

After the getaway was over with, Linda was shocked when the other couple phoned and invited her and Mark to Brands Hatch for a "lovely dinner"

Linda's husband didn't want to go in the first place and grumbled "why do you keep giving out your number to everyone" but went along anyway. They ended up enjoying a "nice dinner" with a number of other married couples who were there too.

But, much to the TV starlets surprise, they weren't all invited just for food and drinks, with the evening taking a very different turn Linda explained: "Eventually we got there three lovely couples sitting and a lovely dinner. And at the end of the dinner all of the car keys went into the middle of the table."

The panel all fell about laughing with Frank Lampard's wife Christine appearing to be horrified as Denise asked: "Who did you get?" Swerving the question, Linda tactfully told her co-stars that Mark made up an excuse that "his brothers Rottweiler had got loose and was ripping up the furniture," with the TV star hilariously adding "your brother hasn't got a Rottweiler," in front of those who were sat at the table.

A few weeks back, the Birds Of A Feather star left ITV viewers a-gasp as she appeared to confirm her 33-year marriage to husband Mark Dunford was done with. However, on today's show she wasn't giving anything away as she continued to refer to him as "my husband".

In September, Linda was discussing matters of the heart on the ITV show alongside co-stars Brenda Edwards, Jane Moore and Denise Welch. But was captivated most attention was her explosive confession.

Jane turned to her co-star asking how things had been at the moment with her love life. Much to everyones surprise, the actress blurted out that she "not looking for anyone" before explaining she "can't be f****".

Linda went on during the same episode: "I've had two loves of my life and now my grandchildren are the love of my life and my children. So no I’m not looking for anyone." Brenda commented: "You’re not looking, you’re not actively looking…"

“No, I can’t be f**** any more," Linda responded bluntly. Leaving Jane to swiftly step in to offer up apologies for Linda's language as co-stars and audience members remained stunned over her admission.
