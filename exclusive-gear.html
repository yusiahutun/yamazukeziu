Splatoween Gear

Nintendo gifted four pieces of 3-star headgear as part of the Splatoween Event (Splatfest#17) in Oct. 2018. The Squid Research Lab Report (Splatoon 2) News Channel on the Nintendo Switch featured a Splatoween article. Players selected a prompt within the article and received the gear upon entering Inkopolis Square. After Splatoween, this gear can only be obtained by finding a player in Inkopolis Square wearing it, and ordering via Murch.
