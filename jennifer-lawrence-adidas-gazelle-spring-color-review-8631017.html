The Comfy Sneakers Jennifer Lawrence and I Rely on Got a Spring Update

Spring’s warm weather is not only an excuse to bring your sundresses and sandals out of hibernation, but it’s also a great time to add color back into your wardrobe. Personally, I’ve been turning to my footwear to brighten up my everyday outfits, and I’m not the only one jumping on the seasonal trend. 

Lately, colorful shoes have been seen all over my home city of Los Angeles, so I finally snagged a vibrant pair of Adidas sneakers in a style spotted on Jennifer Lawrence. While I’ve typically gone for white or neutral sneakers in the past, I had the opportunity to add the pastel green Adidas Gazelles to my footwear collection, and I’ve been surprised by just how versatile this staple shoe has been. 

Adidas Gazelle Shoes

Colorful sneakers are a drastic departure from my go-to choices, but they have added a unique flair to my typical outfit of jeans and a white tee and actually work well when mixed with other bright or bold colors, too. I’ve also found that opting for a colorful sneaker has made it easier for me to pull an outfit together, because they help even the simplest outfits feel more put-together.

Along with adding style to so many of my basic outfits recently, I appreciate that the Adidas Gazelles are durable enough to be an everyday shoe as well. I wore them while traveling and throughout a long day of running errands, and they were so comfortable and supportive, even after walking around in them for hours.

The popular shoes have an average rating of 4.8 stars based on more than 20,000 reviews, so it’s safe to say they hold up. One shopper said that the “quality is truly outstanding,” and another shared that they are “very comfortable,” adding that they “love the style and versatility.” 

Adidas Gazelles come in multiple spring-ready colors, including light purple, blush pink, and mauve, in addition to the light green pair I have. To shop more of these shoes, keep scrolling or head over to Adidas for other options.
