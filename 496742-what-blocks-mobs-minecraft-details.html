What blocks can mobs see through in Minecraft? All the details

A mob, the short form of a mobile entity, is an artificial intelligence-driven game that resembles a living creature. All mobs have some voluntary movement and can be attacked or hurt. Minecraft has different types of mobs with varied artificial intelligence and loot. So, what blocks can mobs see through?

Mobile entities create and place players in various ways in Minecraft. However, most spawn naturally according to the light level, surrounding, and biome. For instance, hostile monsters are placed in the dark, while most animals are placed in bright areas. In addition, most mobs cannot spawn on transparent blocks, on bedrocks, in water, or less than a full-block tall blocks.

What blocks can mobs see through?

There are no such blocks since most mobile entities cannot see through most solid blocks. In addition, these mobile entities cannot see through glass apart from spiders, slimmers, and zombies. However, most mobs are aware of players within 16 blocks or farther away from them.
