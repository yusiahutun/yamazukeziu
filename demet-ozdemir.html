Biography Timeline

Demet Özdemir is a Turkish actress and model who rose to prominence with her portrayal of Sanem in the romantic comedy series “Erkenci Kuş”. She has since become one of Turkey’s most popular and beloved actresses. Born on February 26, 1992, in Kocaeli, Turkey, Demet grew up in a family of modest means. She began her career in show business as a dancer at 17 and later transitioned into modelling. In 2013, she made her acting debut in the TV series “Sana Bir Sır Vereceğim”.
