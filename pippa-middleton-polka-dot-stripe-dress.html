Pippa Middleton Wears a Polka Dot Solid &amp; Striped Dress

  • Pippa Middleton wore a polka dot and striped midi dress during a walk with her son, Arthur, in London yesterday.
  • She completed the look with round sunglasses, white sneakers, and a silver watch.
  • The first-time mom also wore playful prints during her Wimbledon appearances in July.

Pippa Middleton isn't afraid to mix prints.

The younger sister of the Duchess of Cambridge sported both polka dots and stripes on a midi dress during a walk with her son, Arthur, in Chelsea yesterday. The black and white frock, by the Insta-favorite swimwear brand Solid & Striped, features a sweetheart neckline and button details down the front, making it perfect for summer. Middleton completed her casually chic look with white sneakers, a silver watch, and round sunglasses.

Pippa loves a bold print, if you couldn't tell from the lively dresses she wore during her many Wimbledon cameos last month. One of her most notable outfits from this year's tennis competition was a tiered, blue and white tiered floral number by Anna Mason, which she wore while accompanying Kate Middleton and Meghan Markle to the women's singles final.

Karwai Tang//Getty Images

There was also the yellow, sunflower-printed wrap dress by Ganni, which she sported during an appearance with her husband, James Matthews.

Neil Mockford//Getty Images

Aside from her courtside cameos, most of Pippa's fashionable sightings have taken place during her low-key strolls with Arthur, who turns one in October. The mother of one has worn everything from workout attire to statement coats while pushing her baby boy in a stroller.
