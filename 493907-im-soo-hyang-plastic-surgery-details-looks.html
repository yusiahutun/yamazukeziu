Who is Im Soo-hyang?

Im Soo-hyang is a celebrated South Korean actress renowned for various Korean movies and television series. She was born on April 19, 1990, in Busan, South Korea. This means that she is 32 years old as of 2023.

The talented actress attended Chung-Ang University, where she studied theatre and film. Im Soo-hyang made her debut in the movie industry in 2009 and has appeared in numerous movies and TV shows, with big hits under her collection.

Although she entered the movie industry in 2009, getting the recognition she enjoys today took her some time. Her debut in the spotlight and rise in popularity happened after she took part in Korean television series like The Inspiring Generation, released in 2014. Her role in the TV series New Tales of Gisaeng (2011) catapulted her career.

After the two series and their rise in popularity, Im Soo-hyang appeared in other excellent productions like My ID is Gangnam Beauty (2018) and the smash-hit mystery thriller Graceful Family (2019). The actress also won a Korea Drama Award and an SBS Drama Award for her performance in New Tales of Gisaeng in 2011.

In 2022, Im Soo-hyang appeared in the SBS romantic comedy-drama, Woori, based on the American series of the same name. The same year, the actress took part in the MBC medical-legal drama Doctor Lawyer, playing the prosecutor.
