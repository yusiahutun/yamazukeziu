What does Kitty Spencers US$26 million London mansion look like? Princess Dianas niece spent C

A cousin of UK royals Prince William and Prince Harry, Lady Kitty Spencer lives with her fiancé, Michael Lewis, in his US$26 million mansion in central London. Spencer, a model who makes her money through her various modelling contracts, as well as partnerships with Bulgari, Estée Lauder and Dolce & Gabbana, is the eldest child of Charles Spencer, ninth Earl Spencer (she is also a niece of Diana, Princess of Wales). The 62-year-old Lewis – who has an estimated net worth of over US$111 million – is the chairman of the Foschini group, a clothing company in South Africa. So what does the inside of Spencer and Lewis’ home look like – and is it as glamorous as royal residences? More or less, we’d say: the 28-year-old’s Instagram offers plenty of snapshots of the blingy mansion, from the rose-and-crystal bathroom to the gold-heavy lounge.

The big redecoration project

According to the UK’s Evening Standard, Spencer spent a lot of time – and tons of money – redecorating her home during lockdown. She said she didn’t regret spending the money given how much time she had spent indoors because of the coronavirus pandemic.

“I became totally absorbed in the process, from the fabrics to the joinery,” she said in the interview. “I can’t imagine that it’s something I’ll ever regret putting money into. After spending the majority of 2020 at home, I appreciate the space I’ve created now more than ever.”

The lounge

Spencer’s fans got a glimpse of her lounge when she posted a picture of herself posing on a geometric couch. Clearly visible in the background are ornately printed wallpaper, a gold chandelier and a baroque chair.

In another picture, fans can see gold baroque wallpaper offset by crystal wall-hangings. Spencer is holding a book from a bookshelf, giving a peek into the collection of tomes she and Lewis have amassed.

Spencer shared another glimpse of her home when advocating for Justice and Care, which tackles modern slavery as organised crime, via a social media post. In the background of the photo is an artwork in green, white and blue. Spencer is seen posing by the window, which has curtains in soft pink and cream.

Her “chill” area

But Spencer’s home features more casual rooms too, like the one in this image. Here, Spencer is seen in an embroidered pink dressing gown, relaxing on an olive-coloured leather couch – she says one of her favourite things is a cosy Friday night in. The room is done up in mauve curtains, continuing with the pink theme. Beside her is a small round cigar table.

The kitchen

The blonde model, who studied art history in Florence, said in an interview that pizza is her “ultimate guilty pleasure”. Check out this video where she can be seen rolling out pizza dough in her kitchen, which has light-coloured marble counters and elegant wooden cabinets.

The bathroom

You know a bathroom is fancy when it has a chandelier in it. Spencer looks fab in this photo sporting silky lilac PJs. White marble lines the sink area, on which sits a small vase of roses. In another picture, there’s a painting of her pet dog on the window ledge.
