Early Life of Tom Pelphrey

Tom Pelphrey’s full name is Thomas J. Pelphrey. Tom was born on 28th July 1982, in Howell, New Jersey, the United States of America. As of 2023, his age is 41 years old while his horoscope is Leo. Every year, Tom celebrates his birthday on the 28th of July. His father is Richard Pelphrey while his mother is Laurie Pelphrey.

He grew up with his brother, Robert Pelphrey. His maternal grandparents’ names are James N. “Jim” Demgard and Eileen Gelshen. Tom’s paternal grandparents’ names are Joseph Grandison Pelphrey and Jeanne Alice Trowbridge. Tom holds an American nationality while he belongs to mixed ( Danish, English, Irish, Polish, German, Scottish, French, Dutch) ethnical background. He follows Christianity as a religion.
