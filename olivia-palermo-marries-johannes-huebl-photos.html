Olivia Palermo Wears Shorts to Marry Johannes Huebl -- See the Pic!

Olivia Palermo is no stranger to making a fashion statement, so it's no surprise that her wedding dress is causing a lot of buzz!

The 28-year-old style maven tied the knot with German model Johannes Huebl in front of their family and two friends at a park in Bedford, New York, over the weekend.

The brunette beauty didn't choose a traditional wedding gown for her big day. Instead, she pieced together an outfit by Carolina Herrera, which included a cashmere sweater, white shorts and a full tulle skirt with a high slit. She completed her edgy ensemble with a pair of deep blue Manolo Blahnik shoes.

The groom complemented his blushing bride in a white Marc Anthony Hamburg suit.

"The City" alum shared the news of her nuptials on her website, writing, "We really wanted to keep this beautiful day very private and special to us and enjoyed the whole day with our family and two friends. Bedford is such an amazing and romantic countryside."

The couple met through mutual friends back in 2008. Huebl popped the question while the pair was spending the New Year's holiday in St. Bart's.

Congratulations to the newlyweds!
