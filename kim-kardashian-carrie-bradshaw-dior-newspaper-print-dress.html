Kim Kardashian Just Twinned with Carrie Bradshaw in a Dior Newspaper Print Skirt

  • Kim Kardashian West just wore one of Carrie Bradshaw's most famous outfits from Sex and the City.
  • Kardashian rocked the newspaper-print pencil skirt from John Galliano's era at Dior.
  • Bradshaw's version was a figure-flattering dress, rendered in the iconic newspaper print, from Season 3 of the HBO series and in the subsequent 2010 feature film.

Kim Kardashian just threw it back to the year 2000 and channeled Carrie Bradshaw from Sex and the City with help from John Galliano.

Related Story

The reality TV star was spotted in a white tank top and newspaper-print pencil skirt, taken from Galliano's Fall/Winter 2000 collection for Christian Dior, and her inspiration for the look was instantly obvious. Kardashian fully committed to the style, even carrying a matching Dior Saddle bag much like Carrie Bradshaw. (Logomania and the purse's silhouette had a recent resurgence, so Kim's look is both retro and perfectly on trend).

Her most surprising accessory? She threw on a pair of grey flip flops (yes, really!) to complete her throwback look. Kardashian also kept her glam equally as chic with a sleek straight bob, a brown smoky eye, and nude lipstick.

Shutterstock/Getty

In 2000, Carrie Bradshaw donned a Dior newspaper-print dress on Season 3 of SATC. Bradshaw wore the sleeveless, décolletage-baring number when she apologized to Natasha, Mr. Big's wife at the time, for having an affair with him. It was très uncomfortable, but at least she looked chic:

Bradshaw's lewk was so legendary that she brought back the slinky dress for the SATC movie in 2010.
