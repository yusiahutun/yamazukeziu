Liberty Ross Wore Her Wedding Gown To Attend the 2016 Oscars Vanity Fair Party

Getty Images

Liberty Ross recycled the silk charmeuse slip dress with a draped neckline and sweeping train (worn to her pre-nuptial bridal luncheon on the beach) to last night's Oscars festivities. This was just one of Ross's two wedding looks; she donned a black vintage Givenchy gown for the couple's gothic-themed ceremony on February 13. The model and her husband, Jimmy Iovine, showed off their wedding bands as they walked the red carpet at last night's Oscars after-party.

Aside from Kate Middleton's frequent yet chic outfit repeating, it's rare to see a star double up on appearances with an evening gown, especially on Hollywood's biggest night. Livia Firth and Eco Age, who founded the eco-conscious Green Carpet Challenge, applauded the drop-dead gorgeous Ross on her dress recycling via Instagram.
