Former CAA Agent Joe Brauner Joins WME

LONDON (VIP NEWS) — Joe Brauner has joined WME as an agent in the music department, bringing with him longtime clients Norah Jones and Amos Lee.

Brauner was previously a music agent at CAA. Jones and Lee will be represented by WME in North America only; Jones will continue to be represented by CAA outside of North America and Lee will be represented by the Agency Group in Europe. Jones' latest record, "Little Broken Hearts," was released on May 1 and debuted at No. 2 on the Billboard 200. She begins a U.S. tour in June. Lee's fourth studio album, "Mission Bell," debuted at No. 1 on the Billboard 200 in February 2011. Lee is currently writing material for his new album. –According to Billboard
