List of the best camping sites in Naivasha in 2020

Camping in Naivasha is an unforgettable experience, which offers exceptional and friendly activities such as game drives, rock climbing, cycling, guided walks, bird watching, among others. The availability of numerous camping sites in Naivasha makes the search for a suitable site easy. Most of these sites offer the best ambient, fit for relaxing and rejuvenating after a busy week.

Naivasha is strategically located about an hour away from Nairobi. It is, therefore, the best choice for a quick weekend camping trip. A look at some of the best camping sites in Naivasha will help you make an informed decision.

Best camping sites in Naivasha

Domestic tourism is gradually taking centre stage in the lives of Kenyans. A good number of them are often taking holiday vacations or quick weekend trips. Naivasha has excellent relaxing sites you cannot afford to miss.

If you are looking for a place within Naivasha that will give you an amazing camping experience, look no more. Below are some of the best places for camping you should consider visiting.

1. Fisherman’s Camp

Fisherman's Camp is probably the most fascinating camping site in Naivasha. It is located on the shores of Lake Naivasha and extends from a beautiful canopy of acacia trees up into the hills, where you can enjoy breathtaking views of the lake and surrounding areas.

The camp offers a great place to escape the hustle and bustle of the Naivasha town. You can choose to relax at the lake or go on a fantastic Kenyan adventure. Hippopotamuses are often seen roaming about just outside the fenced area.

It is also home to the amazing Rift Valley Festival - a gathering of both local and international music artists, combining traditional African roots and global styles. Fisherman's Camp is undoubtedly the right place for camping in Naivasha, considering the affordable charges.

2. Hell’s Gate National Park

Hell's Gate National Park is a remarkable quarter of the Great Rift Valley. It lies south of Lake Naivasha, and it covers an area of approximately 68.25 square kilometres.

Hell's Gate is characterized by diverse topography and geological scenery, and its an excellent place for camping out in the wild. It has towering cliffs, stark rock towers, water-gouged gorges, belching plumes of geothermal steam, and scrub clad volcanoes.

Some of the incredible activities and thing you can do here include:

  • Bike riding
  • Rock climbing
  • Hiking
  • Adventure walks

3. Naivasha West Beach Camp

This is a premier beach camp located near Kongoni Centre and next to Lake Oloiden. It is approximately 30 kilometres from Naivasha Town, and thus away from the frenzied activities of the town.

The camp has permanent tented camps for all budgets and a large ground for setting up tented camps. It features accommodation, a restaurant, an outdoor swimming pool, a shared lounge, and a garden. High-speed Wi-Fi is also offered throughout the property.

Naivasha West Beach Camp takes your camping experience a notch higher by availing car rental services. You can therefore conveniently access nearby sites such as Hell’s Gate National Park, 9 miles away, or Lake Naivasha National Park, 12 miles away.

4. Lake Naivasha Crescent Camp

Lake Naivasha Crescent Camp is one of the luxurious camps on the shores of Lake Naivasha. The base brings something unique to the camping in Naivasha experience.

It is constructed using canvas and has a capacity of 20 luxury tents. The luxury tents are raised from the ground to give campers amazing views of the surroundings from the private balconies.

Memorable and rejuvenating activities you can enjoy at this camp include:

  • Swimming
  • Boat rides
  • Hiking
  • Game and bird watching
  • Nature walks

5. Fish Eagle Inn

Fish Eagle Inn is a convenient stopover for backpackers and budget travellers as they offer accommodation in both cottages and pitched tents. The camp has expansive grounds that are well maintained.

6. Oloiden Camping Site

Oloiden Camping Site offers a private piece of heaven away from the masses. It is prominently known as one of the finest camping sites in Kenya.

The camping site offers high-quality pitches, with a range of facilities to make your Naivasha camping experience one to remember. Its location next to Lake Oloiden gives an undeniable sense of freedom and unfathomable touring experience.

7. Crayfish Camp

Crayfish Camp is one of those Naivasha camping sites you do not want to miss out on. The camping site is famous because of its fresh crayfish from Lake Naivasha—a must-try if you visit this place.

8. The Sleeping Warrior Lodge and Camp

The Sleeping Warrior Lodge and Camp stands on a lava outcrop commanding stunning views over the private Soysambu Conservancy. The extraordinary beauty of the Soysambu Conservancy by Lake Elementaita with its thousands of flamingos and myriad other bird and animal species lies before you.

Interestingly, the camp has babysitting services. Parents with kids can enjoy their time knowing that their children are well taken care of. You get to enjoy the following activities while at the camp:

  • Bush dining
  • Guided walks
  • Visits to nearby attractions

9. Kiboko Luxury Camp

Kiboko Luxury Camp is a tented camp located on the shore of Lake Naivasha and near Hell's Gate National Park. All guest accommodations feature thoughtful amenities to ensure an unparalleled sense of comfort.

10. Camp Carnelleys

Carnelleys is a tranquil camping site with beautiful bushy grounds located on the shores of Lake Naivasha. Camp Carnelleys offers a wide range of different accommodation, including traditional camping, twin rooms, bandas, and dormitories.
