Nyayo Estate Resident Attacks Female Guard over Spying on Her, Leaking Info to Hubby Abroad

  • A video of a resident of Nyayo Estate raining blows on a female guard at the estate caused a stir online
  • In the video, the lady accused the guard of spying on her and claimed she had been paid by her husband, who was based in the US, to do so
  • She dragged the guard on the floor, telling her to 'marry her man' because she was tired of her spying on her

A video of a woman brutally beating up a female security guard at Nyayo Estate, Embakasi, Nairobi, has gone viral.

Video of woman beating female guard at Nyayo Estate goes viral

In the video that was posted online by blogger Omwamba, the married woman could be heard accusing the guard of spying on her and sending details to her husband.

She beat her up as residents of the estate watched and others recorded on their phones.

"Toa uniform uende uishi kwangu. Hata huna adabu (Remove that uniform and go live in my house. You are shameless!. Do you know me?" the lady said as she dragged her on the ground.

In a police statement seen by TUKO.co.ke, a report made by one of the estate's tenants at the Embakasi police Station reported that the lady lives alone after her husband left the country for the USA after they had a misunderstanding.

"The lady is reported to have approached our guard, Miriam Sifuma asking her if she gives her husband information about her by chatting him. When the guard refused to tell her, she got hold of her and beat her as residents watched. One tried to stop her, but she continued," read the statement.
