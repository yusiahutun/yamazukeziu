RHONY alum Jules Wainstein 'begs' judge to let her see her kids

Jules Wainstein has asked a Florida judge to drop her two children from her ex Michael Wainstein’s restraining order against her.

The 39-year-old RHONY alum was 'begging' the court to 'modify' the existing order barring her from seeing son Jagger, nine, and daughter Rio, seven, according to The Sun on Friday. 

In documents obtained by the publication, Jules asked the Florida court to 'modify/dissolve the temporary injunction as to the children and adopt the New York Post-Trial Decision'.

Her real estate investor ex had Jules added to his original no-contact order following their dispute.

On February 28 her children were removed from her custody, before Jules claimed a New York City judge awarded her sole custody in early March.
