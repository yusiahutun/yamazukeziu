Pamela Andersons Best Makeup-Free Moments in Photos Hollywood Life

The former ‘Baywatch’ star is setting a new trend by going to red carpet events without any added glam. 

Pamela Anderson is promoting a healthier beauty practice — going makeup-free at red carpet and upscale events. We’re accustomed to the lights and the cameras that come with A-lister occasions, and most celebrities pack on the glam while attending. But Pamela has chosen to set this new trend by going bare-faced, and she has become the moment in Hollywood. 

During her 2023 interview with Vogue France, the Baywatch alum explained why she has been showing up to events without any makeup. 

“I don’t have a glam team. I’m just kind of doing this, freestyle,” Pamela explained. “I feel as a woman and a woman of my age, and a woman in the public eye, it’s also your job to be a model of everything. Just all sorts of choices. So, I am just being me, kind of who I am, in all these great clothes running around Paris.”
