AGENCY UPDATES: Ted Kurland Signs Delsener-Managed Jazz Musician The Radiators Signed To Skyline

Ted Kurland Associates has signed jazz singer, pianist, composer Peter Cincotti to an exclusive worldwide booking agreement. Veteran industry promoter cum manager Ron Delsener decided to manage the young jazz musician after seeing him in the lead role in the off-Broadway show, Our Sinatra.

Cincotti will record his first album, to be produced by Phil Ramone, this summer, and to be released on Concord Records in the fall. He and his trio recently completed a 10-show engagement at the Hard Rock Hotel in Las Vegas.

The Radiators Signed To Skyline Music

Sklyline Music has signed The Radiators for exclusive and worldwide booking. Bruce Houghton is the responsible agent.
