Jose Chameleone's Wife Daniella Atim Rocks Female Bestman's Role at Friend's Wedding: &quot;Family&quot;

  • Ugandan music sensation Jose Chameleone's wife, Daniella Atim, took centre stage at a wedding where her friend married the love of his life
  • Atim stood not as a bridesmaid but as the best woman standing proudly beside her friend in a move that defies convention
  • Her decision to step into the role typically reserved for a best man and to do so with flair has sparked conversations and drawn attention for it's against traditional norms

Daniella Atim, the wife of Ugandan musician Jose Chameleone, recently captivated attendees at her friend's wedding by confidently taking up the role of a "best woman."

Why did Atim stand as the best woman?

Traditionally reserved for men, the best man role was expertly filled by Atim, who donned a dapper suit that accentuated her sophisticated style.

The wedding, held over the weekend, as per a Facebook post by Atim, witnessed a remarkable display of camaraderie as she stood proudly by her friend's side.

Jose Chameleone thanks mum for standing by him

Ugandan singer Jose Chameleone shared a post on his social accounts for the first time after being hospitalised at a Kampala hospital.

Chameleone showered his mother, Mrs. Mayanja, with love in a post he shared across his social handles, thanking her for being there for him during his difficult times.

"Thank you Mama, Your love is evident. You have never given up on the truth! I always grow stronger learning from you. God grant you more life," he wrote, accompanied by a photo of him in hospital with his mom standing beside him.

The singer's wife, Daniella Atim, revealed that he was diagnosed with H.pylori.
