All That Matters Announces 2019 Speakers

SINGAPORE (CelebrityAccess) – All That Matters, Singapore’s leading entertainment industry conference and music festival, has announced the speakers for its 2019 edition.

Spanning the entire entertainment mediaverse from music, sports and gaming to digital and marketing, the esteemed list represents a cross-section of more than 200+ speakers that will participate in this year’s event.

Speakers announced thus far include:

Raja Rajamannar, Chief Marketing & Communications Officer, Mastercard
Vinai Venkatesham, MD, Arsenal FC
Javier Tebas, President, LaLiga
Lee Trink, CEO, FaZe Clan
Janice Lee, MD, PCCW Media
Nicole McMillan, VP Marketing, Mars
David Porter, VP – Global Media, Unilever
Willard Ahdritz, Founder & CEO, Kobalt
Mitch Glazier, Chairman & CEO, Recording Industry Association of America
Chris Lee, Board of Directors/A&R Executive, SM Entertainment
