The best hotels in Belfast for 2024

Near Lisburn Road shopping area and the Queens Quarter in South Belfast, The Malone Hotel is a boutique hotel with stacks of contemporary Irish style. Opened as The Bowness hotel in 1945, the grand Victorian townhouse is a short walk from the Botanic Gardens and Queen's University, while Titanic Belfast is a 15-minute drive.

The rooms and suites are delightfully decorated in a light, contemporary style. The best ones are the executive rooms which are larger in size and have hints of its period origins. A generous breakfast is served in the Gallery Restaurant, where dinner is also well worth staying around for. And don't miss an Irish craft cocktail served in The Greyhound, the Malone's modern Irish pub.
