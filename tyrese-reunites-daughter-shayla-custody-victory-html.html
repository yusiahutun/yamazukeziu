Tyrese reunites with daughter Shayla after custody victory

Tyrese Gibson reunited with his daughter Shayla for celebratory sushi at Nobu Malibu on Saturday after a judge awarded him 50/50 custody of the 10-year-old in Los Angeles Court.

The 38-year-old actor-singer wore one of his $34 Shayla Rocks black hoodies with leather pants and told TMZ: 'It's amazing, man. Gotta come out here and enjoy the ocean.'

The six-time Grammy nominee's eight-year-long custody battle for his princess came to an end after he was cleared of all his ex-wife Norma Mitchell's abuse allegations.
