Christina Hall's poolside photo at $12million mansion will leave you lost for words

The HGTV star lives in Newport Beach with her three children and her husband Joshua Hall 

    Christina Hall put her sensational mansion front and center on Instagram on Friday when she revealed the latest addition to her expansive abode. 

    The star shared a video and photos of her two sons, Brayden, seven, and Hudson, three, playing in the backyard.

    The little boys were delighted to discover a giant inflatable car had arrived for them to play with on the water, and while the glimpse into her life was cute - her pool almost eclipsed all else. 

    Situated next to the perfectly manicured lawn, the huge swimming pool was surrounded by trees and an ultra-modern seating area, making it look like a 5-star hotel. 

    This isn't the first glimpse of her impressive outdoor space that Christina has given her social media followers. Earlier in the week, they were blown away by another purchase for her boys and her daughter, Taylor, 12, too.
