Anya Taylor-Joy shows off wedding ring while out with 'husband' Malcolm McRae in Los Angeles

Anya Taylor-Joy was spotted showing off what appears to be her wedding ring while out and about with her partner and apparent husband Malcolm McRae.

The 27-year-old actress was spotted out in Los Angeles with her 29-year-old partner McRae, who were first spotted kissing back in May 2021.

The actress certainly looked quite happy, which comes a week after a rather somber Taylor-Joy was seen being comforted by McRae. 

Taylor-Joy was seen smiling while walking in Los Angeles wearing a brown sweater, while seemingly flashing her ring by holding it up to her chest.

The blonde actress also had a black purse slung over her right shoulder, while carrying a white sweater.

She also stepped out with a matching brown thigh-high skirt and completed her look with black flat shoes.

Her partner Malcolm McRae was seen wearing a grey and blue baseball cap with a white tank top tucked into light blue jeans held up by a black belt. 

He completed his look with black speakers while carrying something to their vehicle for the outing.

Taylor-Joy and McRae were first spotted kissing in New York City in May 2021, just ahead of her hosting debut on Saturday Night Live.

Later that month, she spoke about having a partner in an interview with Elle, though she didn't mention McRae by name.

They made their official red carpet debut at the Vanity Fair Oscars after-party in March 2022, just months before reports surfaced they had secretly married.

Taylor-Joy was seen arriving in Sydney, Australia in late June, with witnesses claiming she flashed her ring at the driver.

Then in July reports claimed they had secretly tied the knot, though neither Taylor-Joy or McRae have confirmed those reports.

Taylor-Joy is coming off what is now the biggest movie of the year, Universal's The Super Mario Bros. Movie, voicing Princess Peach, which has grossed $574.2 million domestic and $1.356 billion worldwide.

She is also coming off a trio of 2022 movies, The Northman, The Menu and Amsterdam, plus episodes of Peaky Blinders.
