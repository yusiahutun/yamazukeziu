Watch This Face: Gigi Ringel

Caoimhe Hahn

Every week, Bazaar's booking team highlights one name to note, from the industry's best-known faces to girls who are just starting out. This week, we turn our attention to Gigi Ringel.

Down-to-Earth beauty Gigi Ringel has a natural, relaxed style, emphasised by her eighties-style curls and bouncy bangs. Her social-media accounts hint that she is rarely not on a beach maintaining her gorgeous golden glow, posing in super-chic seaside looks brought to life with seashell accessories. It’s hardly surprising that the Dutch model has an adoring audience of Instagram followers.

Gigi shows off her enviable, dewy skin and bohemian style in her editorial work, which boasts names such as Cosmopolitan, Grazia and Elle to name a few. “Gigi has a modern, effortless beauty” says Victoria Rich at Wilhelmina Models. “She always has such an amazing positive energy about her, despite her often gruelling schedule, which makes her a total pleasure to work with.”

Most recently, Gigi shot campaigns with the Australian swimwear brand Fella and the Dutch denim brand Lois Jeans. At just 20 years old, the future is looking bright for Gigi and, whether she is on the sandy beaches of Byron Bay or dancing to disco music in platforms and sequins, we can’t wait to see what comes next.

We talk to Gigi about her life in Amsterdam, yoga addiction and dreams of shooting a luxury-perfume campaign.

Caoimhe Hahn

How and when were you discovered?

“It was when I went to the open day of an art academy. A student came up to me and asked if I wanted to walk for their show during Amsterdam Fashion Week. After the show I was so full of adrenaline that I wanted to do it more often. I made my younger sister take some photos and send them to a few agencies. Within a few days I was visiting five agencies with my mum.”

If you weren't modelling, what career would you pursue?

“I would love to have my own beach café, where I'd serve homegrown seasonal food. But I recently discovered my interest for naturopathy, too. Or, it would be back to my all-time dream to become a fashion designer. I might just do it all!”

Caoimhe Hahn

What would you say has been the highlight of your career so far?

“I always think I am in my biggest moment right now, but then next month it feels bigger again and again. Haha, I guess that's a good thing.”

How do you spend your free time?

“I call it 'home retreat'. Just doing all my rituals, going to the gym, yoga, going for walks with my boyfriend, cooking dinners for family and friends, visiting markets. I also recently started designing with one of my best friends. We get together and paint and draw when we both have some time off.”

Caoimhe Hahn

Do you have any travel tips for those visiting your hometown?

“I live in Apeldoorn, an hour away from Amsterdam. Not many tourists go here, but I always tell them to go for a bike ride to the national park. Bring along some food and drinks and go for a picnic at 'de zandverstuiving', which means 'sand drift'.”

What are the three products you can’t live without?

“Weleda Skin Food mixed together with Pai Rosehip Oil and my Red Square lipstick from Nars.”

What’s your favourite form of exercise?

“I’m so addicted to yoga. I start almost every day with either a full yoga session or, when I have just a little time, a few stretches. The hip stretch is my favourite – it’s crazy how sore it can be sometimes, so it feels great to relieve that spot. Oh and I can be made really happy with some disco jams and a boogie.”

Caoimhe Hahn

What is your dream shoot?

"I would love to do a big perfume campaign or commercial. Crazy acting scenes, dancing around in big dresses: yes."

Name the five essential items you wouldn’t leave the house without

"Phone, wallet, keys, sunnies, lip balm."

If you had to wear only one designer, who would it be?

“Baum Und Pferdgarten – I am obsessed.”

What advice would you give to new girls to the industry?

“I still tell myself this whenever I need to: pinch yourself now and then to remind yourself that you’re so lucky with anything that comes your way, the opportunities you get and the people you get to meet. What you create, but also that people love you most when you love who you are.”

Is there a model’s career that you admire?

“Jeanne Damas – she’s such a natural beauty and icon.”

What is your life motto?

“Do not try to make it perfect, try to make it interesting.”

Let’s talk about favourites. What about…

…music? “Tora – Another Case.”

…movies? “The Prestige.”

…books? “The Things You Can See Only When You Slow Down by Haemin Sunim.”

…food? “Middle Eastern.”
