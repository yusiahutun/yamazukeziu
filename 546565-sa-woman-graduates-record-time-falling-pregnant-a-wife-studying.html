Woman Graduates in Record Time Despite Falling Pregnant and Becoming a Wife While Studying

  • A woman opened up about the curveballs she faced while at university
  • She said that she got pregnant during her second year, married at 21, and even moved in with her in-laws
  • Despite the unexpected turns, the woman still graduated on time and expressed having no regrets about how her life

A young woman has shared a TikTok video detailing how her life took several unexpected turns, but she still managed to achieve her goal of graduating from university.

Lady juggles motherhood and education

Khanyisa (@khanyisa_000) shared a post of herself looking beautiful in her graduation cap and hood. She disclosed that she fell pregnant while doing her second year and got married at the age of 21.

Khanyisa also moved in with her in-laws and still graduated in record time.
