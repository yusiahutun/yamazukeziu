How to style fluffy kinky braids

1. Twisted fluffy kinky style

Fluffy kinky twist is a delightful African hairdo accounting for its immense popularity in the region. The thickness of the twist vary considerably depending on one’s preferences. With these full looking kinky twists it is almost always by default that you will become noticeable the moment you show up.

How to style

Start by brushing your hair then parting it carefully to form rounded or square sections based on your preferred looks. Take some kinky twist hair pieces and braid them down securely. Now take a couple of strands and be sure to twist them to the right while holding the remaining strand on your left hand. Continually overlap the two strands to the end. Seal the ends by wrapping them between your fingers. After achieving afro kinky twists, you can simply style them by holding them loosely as if you are braiding one line in the middle.

2. Fluffy kinky braids long

The style is handy in expressing the strength and gorgeous looks of any African woman daring enough to try them. These braids are simple yet stunning for most parts not only for their appropriate length but also flaunting outlook.

Long fluffy kinky braids give sufficient room to experiment with several looks. Make rounded or square sections depending on your desired end look. Braid your hair to realize long fluffy kinky braids, then you can leave them falling at the back, or you can try tying them in a big loose bun at the center as shown below. For the bun, neatly arrange the braids at the middle then rotate them in one direction to form a neat bun.

3. Natural fluffy kinky hairdo

Not very many ladies are lucky to find hair stylists with a mastery of varied hairdo skills. For this reason, natural fluffy style is accessible and easy to plait besides a count of benefits as a protective hairstyle.

How to style

Braid your hair entirely from the front to the back. Braid much smaller lines where you want to have your closure and openings as illustrated below.

Now untwist your fluffy kinky then divide each of them to form five pieces. It is crucial to do the division to ease entry into the bobby pin or the passage between the braids. Using a couple of bobby pins, place one between your braids and another pinned into a piece of fluffy kinky. Remove the pin when the fluffy kinky passes through the braid then tie a knot carefully to realize such incredible looks.

4. Fluffy twist crochet braids

Twisted fluffy kinky crochet braids are fashionable among Kenyan ladies for their beautiful skin tone. The style is easy to achieve however intricate when trying it for the first time.

How to style

Carefully section and braid your hair. Pick about 8 to 9 inch of synthetic hair to attain your preferred looks. It is always a good decision to install the right pattern of fluffy afro twist hair for impressive looks.

5. Hot crochet fluffy kinky braids

Hot crotchets are fancy and elaborate featuring in several categories as a favorite African style. The style looks even better with the right makeup and accessories on. Hot crotchet braid is a fun protective style that guarantees unlimited looks.

How to style

Braid your hair from side to side making legible lines to plait fast you fluffy hair. Now style your puffy locs then apply colorful beads to enrich your final looks.

6. Fluffy kinky braids with cornrows

The style differs significantly from other crotchet kinky styles and is now popular in most streets across major Kenyan towns. Done with the right makeup, the style is a versatile head turning charm that will rock any occasion.

How to style

The style is largely unique in both styling and final looks. Install the cornrows on either the front or the sides of your head depending on your desired final impression. Cornrows are the best part of the hairdo for their uniqueness. It is helpful to note that the cornrows play a central role in protecting the hairline.

The style is among the most accessible and easy to plait fluffy kinky hairdo.

Start by crocheting your hair then straightening it under low and controlled heat using a blow drier. You should gently straighten to avoid pulling off the lines sitting on the inside. After straightening, you can style this hair by plaiting neat crown braids as shown in the image below.
