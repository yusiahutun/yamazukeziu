Hailey Bieber reveals how she grew her confidence as she debuts 'beautiful' new look

Hailey Bieber has taken to the cover of V Magazine this September, as she channels a new look, stunning in smart business wear.

In the cover shoot, which comes out weeks after Hailey faced pregnancy rumors, the star talks about her modeling career, and how she’s grown her confidence over the past decade.

Hailey Bieber knows what she brings to the table

The model opened up to V Magazine about her modeling career, which started when she was just 16 years old. A decade later, she’s taken part in endless catwalks, including the world-famous Victoria’s Secret.

As well as her highly successful modeling career, Hailey launched her own skincare line, Rhode, in 2022. Starting out and becoming so well known at a young age, Hailey revealed to the publication how she grew her confidence over those years.

“The inner dialogue of how I speak to myself, and how I encourage myself, in my own head has been something that’s been really important for me.

“Confidence can grow over time, and I think that’s been the case for me. I understand what I’m good at and what I can bring to the table. And I know that more now than I ever have before,” she concluded.

Fans call Hailey the ‘It Girl’

Taking to her 50 million Instagram followers, Hailey posted the cover of the magazine, where she stunned in a striped navy blazer and a low-cut white shirt.

The 26-year-old topped the look off with a slicked back hair-do and bright red lipstick, as fans went wild for the new look in the comments.
