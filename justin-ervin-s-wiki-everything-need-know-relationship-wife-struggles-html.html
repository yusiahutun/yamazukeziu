Justin Ervin's Wiki: Age, Height, Married Life, Wife, Baby

EntertainmentBy LRU Writer | On: January 21, 2020

African-American filmmaker, director, and cinematographer Justin Ervin is best known for his documentary, Elephant In The Room (2012), which won him several accolades. His other prominent works include Six Months Later (2013) and Peach Fuzz (2016). 

He is also the owner of Element Films Inc, which also goes by the name Justin Ervin Films. The company solely focuses on photography, digital media, and movie making.

The filmmaker has also worked with some high profile clients such as Netflix, Adobe, Guess, CNN, The Gap, Pantene, Swimsuits for All, Staples, and Aspire TV.

Justin Ervin's Married Life, Baby

Justin has been married to model Ashley Graham since 2010.
