She typically appears in sexy costumes on-screen, but Kate Beckinsale has always stopped short of going naked.

That all may change, however - the 38-year-old Underworld: Awakening actress says she would consider appearing in the buff on the big screen.

There's a condition behind her decision - it must be for a role that her daughter would be proud of.

Cover girl: Kate Beckinsale tells Redbook magazine that she hasn't ruled out posing naked

'I think it's worse nowadays, because you do something [like a nude scene] and somebody immediately makes a five-times as long feature on YouTube to rock music, and they play it over and over again,' the brunette beauty says in the January 2012 issue of Redbook magazine.

She explains: 'I'm not averse to doing a nude scene, but it would have to be something I felt I could really argue in front of my daughter, and so far that hasn't happened.'

Lily, 12, is her child with ex-husband Michael Sheen; she is now married to her first Underworld director, Len Wiseman.

Of her current husband she tells the magazine: 'We're just a good couple. Having a husband who's comfortable being very affectionate is important.'

'Women do well with that. If the husband is very loving, the wife blossoms, and everybody's fine.'

Redbook hot: The 38-year-old brunette beauty looks gorgeous in winter white as Redbook's latest cover girl

Beckinsale was married to Sheen for eight years; their relationship deteriorated in 2003 when she and Wiseman fell in love on the set of their vampire thriller.

All parties have maintained that there was no infidelity, and all three remain friends today.

She continues to talk about the importance of family in relation to how she spends her time when she isn't working.

'I was at home. We’ve got a kind of unconventional family situation, with two dads, and I’ve always been the constant parent, the one who is here. Even if I’m working, it’s me,' she explains.

She adds: 'So if anything ripples the water, then it’s important to be home. My daughter is about to be 13, and I realize how fleeting time is. I was happy to be around Lily to talk about her day when she got home.'

Given that the British stunner is a woman who knows what she wants when it comes to both love and her career, so it isn't surprising that she should gravitate towards action roles.

She has appeared in stunt-heavy films like Van Helsing, Vacancy and the upcoming action movie, Contraband.

Kate's two loves: The Van Helsing actress shares her life with daughter Lily and husband Len Wiseman

Of her attraction to these tough girl roles she says: '[They're an] incredible opportunity. Here I am with a gun; here I am on a wire; here I am learning gymnastics.'

'I remember turning up in Budapest my first day of filming, running along a subway with a couple of guns. I thought I was going to die, I was so scared. But I very much wanted to play roles that were not just the girl taking a shower, the girl who was getting beaten, or just the girl.'

'Now I'm someone who goes to Comic-Con--someone who knows what Comic-Con is!'

The proud star certainly looks amazing whatever she's wearing, whether it happens to be leather, camouflage or glamorous gear.

She appears on the Redbook cover looking elegant despite the short hemline of her white minidress.

She continues to look elegant throughout the spread in black and white ensembles including a white trench, black leggings and motorcycle boots and a black dress with a flower applique at the shoulder.
