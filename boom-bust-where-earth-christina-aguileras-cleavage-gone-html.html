The 27-year-old singer showed off her modest curves during a night out with husband Jordan Bratman in Hollywood on Thursday.

Dressed in a plunging black halterneck maxi dress and a crochet cardigan, Aguilera looked stunning at the launch of the L'Agence Clothing and the Fashion Rules board game at Fred Segal.

As recent as September, when Aguilera was visiting London, she was still cutting a busty figure.

The Genie In A Bottle singer revealed she had started breast-feeding Max the day he was born in January and she was in 'awe' of the process.

She said: '(The first time) it was magical. It's amazing: Your body is this supplier of food. I'm in awe of it.'

Keeps Gettin' Smaller: Aguilera cut a slimmer figure in a maxi dress on Thursday (L), and a bustier Aguilera at the MTV Video Music Awards in September (R)

When she appeared dressed in a low-cut top on comedienne Ellen DeGeneres talk show in February, her cleavage was an obvious topic of conversation.

DeGeneres said: 'You look great. One question: Are you nursing?'

Aguilera responded: 'I guess it's a little obvious.'

DeGeneres replied: 'It's going to be a healthy baby boy.'

Aguilera put on 40 pounds while pregnant with Max and went up two shoes sizes.

Skinny star: A slimmer Aguilera at the 2000 Grammys long before marriage and babies

She expected her breasts to shrink after his birth, but was amazed when they continued to swell while she was breastfeeding.

She said in May: 'It's kind of hilarious! I've never fit into an E-cup before.

'I look at my husband and go, "Guess what size this bra is?" And when I tell him, he's just amazed. We have kept the tags that prove it, to look back for memory's sake.

'My stomach was really where I carried my weight. Toward the end of my pregnancy, I felt really big - I gained about 40 pounds, which is a lot for my size.

'My hips also changed significantly. And my feet grew from a size five-and-a-half to a size seven in flats.'

The singer quickly lost the pregnancy weight through breast-feeding, but her chest remained large.

She said: 'I think they say that when you're breastfeeding, you know, your weight kind of slims down.

'It's a little easier. It's like a workout within itself. It's very tiring actually and you find yourself snacking more often.

'Thank God my feet went back to normal. I would have been so depressed if I had to get rid of my shoe collection.'

Aguilera is currently promoting her new greatest hits album Keeps Gettin' Better - A Decade Of Hits and is set to release a new studio album next year.
