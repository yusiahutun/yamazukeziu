Lady Gaga Is in Her Rockstar Era With a Mick Jagger-Inspired Jumpsuit

Lady Gaga is a one-of-a-kind artist and performer, and not to mention, a force to be reckoned with on the fashion front (see: her scene-stealing Grammy looks over the years). And yesterday, she somehow managed to up her cool factor even further with an outfit that was rock-n-roll to its core.

On Thursday, the singer-slash-actress was photographed out and about in New York City after her surprise performance with The Rolling Stones during their Hackney Diamonds album release party. For the performance, Gaga went full-on rocker chick in a red and black two-toned jumpsuit that was undoubtedly inspired by the band's frontman, Mick Jagger. Gaga's kick-flare catsuit was covered in sequins and came complete with a plunging deep-V neckline, a single sleeve, and flared bell bottoms. A pair of sky-high platform heels, several layered necklaces, and a black sparkly necktie provided the finishing touches to her look.

For her glam, she opted for a dramatic siren eye with a super glossy pink lip, strong brows, a bronzy contour, and a black manicure. But what really stole the show on the beauty front was her brand-new platinum blonde shoulder-length shag. She styled her abbreviated tresses with tousled, choppy layers and forehead fringe.

Her outing came shortly after she paired her newly '70s-inspired chop with an outfit that was reminiscent of Karen Sirko (played by Suki Waterhouse) in Daisy Jones and the Six's. On Wednesday, Gaga was photographed outside Electric Lady Studios in a sheer ivory ruffled blouse layered over a black bra and under a burnt orange blazer with black lapels. She accessorized with a pair of black velvet hot pants, coordinating knee-high boots, a black handbag, and chunky square sunglasses.
