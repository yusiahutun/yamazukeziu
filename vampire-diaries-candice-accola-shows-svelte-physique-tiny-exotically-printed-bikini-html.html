She may play a vampire on television, but on Tuesday Candice Accola showed she wasn't afraid to venture into the sunlight.

The 26-year-old showed off her slim and tanned figure in a multicoloured bikini while enjoying a spring and sunny holiday in Maui on Tuesday.

The blonde actress appeared luxuriously relaxed and blissful while strutting her svelte stuff alongside her fiance, Fray musician Joe King, 33.

What a figure! Candice Accola strolled on the beach in her tiny, multicoloured bikini while in Maui on Tuesday with her fiance Joe King

The trim Vampire Diaries star was captured strolling along the sandy Maui shores in a colourful bikini that hugged her trim figure, all while showing off her exotic style in a bathing suit embellished with what appeared to be a marijuana leaf.

In an attempt to stay protected from the spring sun, Accola kept her face covered up behind large and fashionable sunglasses, and contained her wavy blonde mane within a fashionable straw fedora.

Accola appeared content and blissful while striding barefoot upon the damp sand, giving a slight smile as she trotted in the direction of the awaiting beach chairs.

Looking fresh faced! Accola sported a healthy and radiant tan, and appeared to have opted for a make-up free complexion

Sifting through sand: The Vampire Diaries actress appeared luxuriously relaxed while striding upon the soft Maui sands

The actress appeared to face the beach day with fresh complexion, opting to show off her radiant and tanned glow with minimal make-up assistance.

Fray musician King was captured emerging from the Hawaiian waters with his wet hair slicked back, all while sporting a healthy tan in navy blue trunks.

Like his fiance, King appeared to have put in time at the gym, and showed off his well-toned midsection while parading upon the sand.

There he is! Joe King emerged from the Hawaiian waters, showing off his toned midsection in a pair of navy blue trunks

Things are looking up! The blonde beauty kept her chin up as she walked in the direction of the beach chairs

The Fray will be venturing on a tour at the end of April, and it appeared King was relishing a last-minute romantic getaway with Accola just before his exhaustive excursion.

The actress took to social media to share her Hawaiian excitement, captioning a photo of herself sprawled upon a hammock, 'Hanging out in heaven... Maui #FSMaui #MaiTaiTime#bliss.'

Accola currently stars alongside Nina Dobrev and Paul Wesley in the hit CW series, The Vampire Diaries.

The series was recently renewed for a sixth season in February, perhaps giving Accola another reason to luxuriously lounge in a belated celebration.
