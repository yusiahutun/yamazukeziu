Christina Ricci gives peek of breast tattoo in plunging black velvet gown on glamorous Emmys 2024 re

Christina Ricci flashed a glimpse of her sparrow tattoo on her cleavage in a plunging gown as she made an appearance at the 75th Annual Emmy Awards in L.A. on Monday. 

The Yellowjackets star, 43 - who recently exuded gothic glamour while at the Critics Choice Awards - joined other celebrities at the glamorous event, such as Padma Lakshmi and Camila Morrone. 

The beauty showcased her stunning figure wearing a fitted, black velvet dress that contained a deep, plunging neckline on the front. 

A peek of her tattoo - which she got around 20 years earlier - could be seen on her right breast. Sparrows notably signify freedom, loyalty as well as love.  

The ensemble was held up with thick straps that wrapped around her shoulders while the hem flowed down straight to brush the ground.

Ricci accessorized her look with a pair of dangly, silver earrings as well as an eye-catching, silver-chained necklace with a sparkling green pendant. 

She also added a matching bracelet on her right wrist as well as a flashy ring that was placed on her left hand. 

The Addams Family actress easily carried a black, Yves Saint Laurent clutch bag to hold a few items she needed during the festivities. 

Her makeup was glammed up for the big night, and comprised of a layer of mascara to her lashes as well as a smoky shadow around her eyes. 

A warm blush and contour was added to better accentuate her cheekbones, while a nude-colored matte tint was worn on her lips for a finishing touch. 

Christina was spotted confidently striking a variety of poses on the red carpet before heading into the Peacock Theater ahead of the ceremony - which was hosted by Anthony Anderson. 

Her latest show, Yellowjackets, notably garnered three Emmy nominations at the event - including Outstanding Drama Series and Outstanding Lead Actress in a Drama Series for Ricci's co-star Melanie Lynskey. 

The Showtime project also raked in a nod for Outstanding Casting for a Drama Series.

While at the 29th Annual Critics Choice Awards that were held one day earlier on Sunday, the actress opened about what the upcoming third season might have in store for fans. 

The star admitted to Entertainment Tonight that she knows 'nothing' about the plot of the next season, and explained that, 'They do not tell us anything.' 

'The only thing that we know is that there are certain things that happened in the second season that clearly are going to have to be addressed, so you can make some sort of like, assumptions.' 

'But it's the kind of show where anything can happen and it's wild and so who knows, really,' the beauty added. 

She also revealed that a 'stand alone episode' of Yellowjackets has also been filmed that 'doesn't involve any of the regular cast.' 

'And I believe that they are going to put that out as a stand alone, kind of special event,' Ricci continued, but then quickly added, 'But I'm also not 100 percent sure...'
