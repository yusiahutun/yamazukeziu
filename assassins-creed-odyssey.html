  • Writhing Dead

    • Group: Shadows of Serpents

    Category: Odyssey Quest

    Having opened the gate, Alexios rushed in with Bryce to find Ligeia and fight the Writhing Dread.

    Related points of interest

    Petrified Temple World Map

    Prior quests

    To begin Writhing Dead you must first complete A Slithery Plea.

    Objectives of Writhing Dead

    1. Open the lair of the Writhing Dread

    2. Track Bryce through the petrified temple

    3. Kill Medusa

    Kill adds to remove her shield and make her vulnerable to damage. Avoid her sight (orange energy beam) by hiding behind the pillars asap and avoid her another attack (red circles on the ground) by moving a lot. Repeat until she is dead ;-) IMO, a good bow is the best weapon here.

    4. Acquire the First Civilization Artifact

    This is one of the artifacts that you need to find in order to complete The Gates of Atlantis.

    5. Quest Completed

    Rewards: Legendary XP, Legendary Drachmae, Prize of Medusa (Quest Item), Medusa Figurehead, Harpe of Perseus (Legendary Sword)
