Tommy Emmanuel Announces 2019 US Tour Dates

Tommy Emmanuel. Credit: Alysse Gafkjen.


(CelebrityAccess) – Esteemed Grammy-nominated guitarist, composer and performer Tommy Emmanuel has today (Feb. 27) announced an array of U.S. headline tour dates.

Scheduled to kick off July 15th in Homer New York, the tour will make stops in New London, CT, Oklahoma City, OK, Kansas City, MO, and Cincinnati, OH among others, before wrapping up in Frankfort, KY on September 28th. Emmanuel will be joined by Jorma Kaukonen, Mike Dawes and Joe Robinson on various dates.

In 2018, Emmanuel’s Accomplice One collaborations album earned him the No. 1 spot on Billboard’s “Bluegrass Albums” chart. The album also debuted in Top 5 on Billboard’s “Heatseekers Albums,” Top 15 on “Independent Albums” and Top 20 on the “Americana/Folk” chart alongside its No. 1 debut on the iTunes “Jazz Albums” chart.

Tickets for the tour go on sale this Friday, March 1: https://tommyemmanuel.com/tours/
