24 perfumes celebrities actually wear (but arent paid to)

Beyoncé

While in London for the UK leg of her Renaissance tour, Beyoncé reportedly snuck into the Harrods beauty hall after hours to stock up on some favourites. Her scent of choice has been revealed to be Angels' Share by Kilian: a classic from the brand's liquor-themed line-up.

An ode to the heady fruit notes of cognac – and a nod to founder Kilian Hennessy's family business – the gourmand fragrance blends warm vanilla and cinnamon spice over a smooth base of sandalwood, making for a truly intoxicating trail.

Jodie Comer

The Liverpudlian actress has mentioned her staple perfume is none other than Le Labo Santal 33. "I was working with someone years ago who wore it and I thought it was delicious, so I copied her – and now it's mine!" she admitted, laughing, in an episode of Inside My Beauty Bag. "I'd always know where she'd been in the hotel or where she was in the vicinity because I always smelt her before I saw her. I think it smells different on everyone"

Hailey Bieber

Elizabeth Olsen

Skincare enthusiast Elizabeth Olsen is a fan of subtler fragrances, particularly Diptyque's Philosykos – a creamy fig scent. "I'm not one for perfumes, they often give me a headache," she tells Bazaar, which is why a roll-on or balm is a great alternative as it sits closer to the skin. They also often smell gentler than their spray-on counterparts. "I prefer the fig from Diptyque and that's the only kind of scent I use."

Leaving a soft amber vanilla fragrance in its wake, we can see why Adele has kept this perfume on her dressing table all this time.

The bottle in question was recently revealed to be Love, Don't Be Shy by Kilian: a sweet, suitably decadent gourmand blend of neroli and marshmallow. (Although, no doubt she wears her own Fenty Eau de Parfum now, too.)

Since then she's been inseparable from the scent, which has notes of vanilla, tonka bean and myrrh; a perfect fragrance for autumnal evenings.
