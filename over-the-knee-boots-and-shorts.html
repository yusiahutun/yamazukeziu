How to Wear Over The Knee Boots + Shorts Without Looking Too Sexy

Media Platforms Design Team

We're all for being daring when it comes to fashion choices—but pairing our weekend cutoff shorts with leather over-the-knee boots conjures up thoughts of Julia Roberts in Pretty Woman. However, after seeing Russian street-style star Elena Perminova front row at the Chanel Haute Couture show in Paris last week in the above ensemble—we started rethinking this unusual pairing. Her expert styling makes this a great transitional look from summer to fall. Perminova tames this otherwise sultry look by adding volume on the top with a fun oversized sweater, and floppy hat.
