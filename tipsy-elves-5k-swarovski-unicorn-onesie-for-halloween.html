Tipsy Elves $5k Swarovski Unicorn Onesie for Halloween

Tipsy Elves and Swarovski have teamed up to create a Halloween costume that redefines extra – a Swarovski crystal studded unicorn onesie!

Valued at $5000, this cozy unicorn onesie will certainly turn heads, thanks to almost 2500 crystals (including 220 in just the horn!), this costume will help ensure that you’re the brightest, shiniest member of your squad this Halloween.

In honor of Breast Cancer Awareness Month, all proceeds from the onesie will be donated to Susan G. Komen. So if you have some extra cash, be extra this Halloween…while being charitable, too!
