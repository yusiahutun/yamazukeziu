Are Ryan Seacrest and Aubrey Paige Petcosky Still Together?

Ryan Seacrest seemed to have found his most serious relationship yet with model Aubrey Paige Petcosky. Are they still together?

How Did Ryan Seacrest Meet Aubrey Paige?

The couple first sparked dating rumors during Memorial Day weekend in May 2021. They took a trip to the Hamptons and were photographed together when they returned to New York City. That June, Closer confirmed Ryan and Aubrey were an item. Despite their 23-year age gap, the couple fell hard for each other. 

“Safe to say the best part of 2021 was meeting the most incredible man,” Aubrey gushed in a New Year’s Eve Instagram post, adding, “Wishing everyone love, happiness, safety and health in 2022.”

Six months later, the pair was spending the summer in the Hamptons, where they shared their love of cooking. “Hello and welcome to our test kitchen … where two people obsessed with food try to teach other how to cook. A great weekend always involves cooking with a view. Happy Sunday!! [sic]” the Delaware native wrote next to a photo of the lovebirds cuddled up on their patio followed by a series of snapshots of the meal they were making.  

Are Ryan Seacrest and Aubrey Paige Still Together?

Aubrey shared an Instagram video filled with cozy photos of the couple on February 14, 2023. “Happy Valentine’s Day mi amor, ” Aubrey wrote to Ryan. “Loving you has been the adventure of a lifetime. Cheers to many more laughs, sunrises, meals, and spontaneous adventures. Blessed to have the honor of loving you,” she added.

However, months later, Ryan did not appear in Aubrey’s holiday photos shared on her Instagram account. “Where’s Ryan!?” one person asked in a comment under a post shared to Aubrey’s page on Christmas. She did not address the comments asking about Ryan’s whereabouts during the holiday celebration.

Will Ryan Seacrest and Aubrey Paige Get Engaged?

The Georgia native has opened up about wanting to find love in the past in segments on Live With Kelly and Ryan. He announced his departure from the show after six years on February 16, 2023. Kelly’s husband Mark Consuelos took over in Ryan’s place, with the show rebranded, Live With Kelly and Mark. 

During a 2017 episode of the show when cohost Kelly Ripa asked Ryan if he had ever been close to getting engaged, he remarked that he “did get close.” Now that he’s with Aubrey, it seems like she may be The One.

“Aubrey might be the one who surprises us all and finally gets that elusive proposal from Ryan,” an insider told Closer in 2022.  

Prior to his relationship with Aubrey, he was last linked to wellness influencer Shayna Taylor, whom he dated on and off from 2013 to 2020. A representative for the entertainment mogul announced their breakup in June 2020, noting that they still “remain good friends” and “each other’s biggest supporters.”

Ryan’s most high-profile romance was with Dancing With the Stars alum Julianne Hough. The pair dated from 2010 through 2013. “Every relationship, there was nothing wrong or bad, but there was nothing right,” she told Redbook in 2014 following their split. “I had one foot out because I didn’t want to get hurt. And I didn’t say what was on my mind because I didn’t want to ruffle any feathers.”
