Adam Pally‘s life is an ideal balance of professional success and lovely family life. Pally’s wife, Daniella Liben, and their three children — Cole, Georgia Grace, and Darke — kept him sane during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Pally’s Parenting on Lockdown

In a June 2020 interview with New York Magazine, Pally was happy to share that they were doing pretty well in their pandemic lifestyle as a family. According to the actor, his children are a handful and we’re at the worst age for the pandemic — it was difficult for him to manage three small children at the same time. In April 2020, he also published a journal on Lockdown Parenting in Mr. Porter, describing his typical day.

Furthermore, Pally and Liben fought because neither of them knew how to use new technologies, even though the students were required to use virtual mediums for their classes.

Pally wrote in his Mr. Porter journal about how cleanliness was not his top priority during the lockdown days because he was too busy entertaining the kids until lunchtime. He and his wife would take turns showering while the kids were preoccupied with more food. Despite the fact that his businesses were closed during the pandemic, Pally did not let his children or wife see him panic or cry.

Evenings in quarantine, on the other hand, were blissful. The first shot of tequila was poured after the phone’s alarm went off as the clock struck five, and the couple would play Frozen II and cook dinner together while dancing and having fun. Pally stated that he would miss the quarantine period because it would allow him to be close to the children for such long periods.

Pally and Liben have been married for more than a decade

In July 2008, Pally married Liben. Since then, the couple has been inseparable. Back then, the comedian was just starting out and was known as a “television advertising actor,” and Liben was the owner of Ruby, a women’s boutique in New Jersey.

Pally and other Dog Days cast members answered the most frequently asked questions about them in a 2018 Wired video. Pally’s fans were apparently curious about his wife. Pally gushed, “My wife is my high school sweetheart,” before making a joke about taking credit for their long-term love. What a sweetie!
