Magaluf foam parties go insane as revellers get cheeky under the bubbles

Magaluf is nearing the end of its wild summer season with Brits continuing to live it up – and one party in particular is going off.

Magaluf Events is holding huge foam parties on the Spanish island of Majorca.

With a tagline of “what happens in the foam, stays in the foam” – it’s fair to say it’s full of dirty fun.

For £35, the Tuesday event takes place at Car Wash Club in the middle of the resort, just head to the huge seven-hour event at 11pm.

Snaps from inside the party show lads and lasses beaming at the camera and enjoying the foam.

Two Irish women shared a selfie soaked from the foam, saying: “No Irish, no party.”

While another two joke: “Ugliest yet cutest pic ever.”

A trio of revellers also posted a series of pics from the wild event, saying: “Chillin @ foamy.”

On its website it reads: “When you are in Magaluf it is important to experience a foam party.

"There is no better place than Carwash night club situated at the top of the Magaluf strip.

“Over 5000 gallons of foam each week are poured from cannons onto the dance floor making it extremely messy!

“This event is all inclusive so do not worry about bringing your wallet or purse with you."

And the experience is on you’ll certainly never forget.

The website adds: “Everybody in Magaluf knows that Tuesdays are for foam parties.

“So be ready to experience the ultimate foam party alongside hundreds of other Magaluf partygoers.

“Magaluf nightlife does not get any better than this and remember what happens in the foam stays in the foam.

“Remember you may get very wet so wear clothing suitable to party all night long in the foam.”

Previously speaking to Daily Star Online, one Brit reveller said the nightclub is banging.

He said: “Place is mental.

"Banging party music and crazy half naked games followed by whatever messy thing they have planned for that night.”
