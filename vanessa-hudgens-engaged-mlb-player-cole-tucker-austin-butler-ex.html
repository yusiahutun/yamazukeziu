Vanessa Hudgens is engaged to boyfriend Cole Tucker after two years together

The Tick,Tick, Boom actress has been dating her MLB boyfriend for two years

    The pair first sparked romance rumors in November of 2020, when they were spotted out on a date in Los Angeles.
