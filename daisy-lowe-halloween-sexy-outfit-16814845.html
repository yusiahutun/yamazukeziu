MEOW! Daisy Lowe turns Catwoman in skintight PVC costume

The former Strictly siren regularly wowed with her racy on-stage costumes in the 2016 series of the show.

And Daisy Lowe is showing no signs of slowing down with all those sizzling ensembles, even today.

Stepping out for Fran Cutler's glitzy annual Halloween party at MOMO in Mayfair on Thursday night, the 29-year-old opted for a very sexy costume as she danced the night away alongside her showbiz pals.

Squeezing her ample figure into a skintight black latex suit, the model-turned-DJ channelled her inner cat woman.

Daisy – who is the daughter of Gavin Rossdale – flaunted every inch of her figure in the racy garment, which was cinched in at the waist and clung to her booty.

Having traded in her long raven locks for a blonde blunt bob, Daisy certainly got into character as she seductively ran her hands through her hair while throwing some saucy poses for photographers at the event.

The babe opted for a dark smokey eye and hot red lip combo to contrast with her all-black look.

Meanwhile, the social butterfly threw on a pair of towering stiletto boots and a vampy headpiece for extra sass.

Daisy hit headlines during her time on the BBC show after developing a close bond with her fellow Strictly contestant, singer Louise Redknapp.

The duo were regularly spotted side by side, both on the show and off, while Louise reportedly credited Daisy for helping her deal with her divorce from now-ex husband Jamie.

However the pair were later forced to deny that they were "dating" after rumours began swirling over the status of their close friendship, with some speculating that they were secretly in a same-sex relationship.

Speaking on her Femme podcast, Daisy explained: “I'm aware of all the lesbian hints. It actually makes me laugh.

“People find it strange we are friends. Why? She's an amazing, warm, honest woman. I'm proud of her. This is what women should do, support each other.”

She added: “I don't use best friend often especially with someone I’ve only known for a year but Louise Redknapp completely changed my life and the two of us became these kind of wonderful confidence boosters for one another.”
