Brantley Gilbert's Baby Is Getting a Custom Tour Bus

Brantley Gilbert’s new baby is going to be riding in style. The country star is building a custom tour bus to accommodate not only his new bundle of joy, but his wife, Amber, so the family can tour together.

According to an article on Rare Country, Gilbert’s first attempt to have the bus built was not a pleasant one. “We’re having a bus built right now and we just had one built that sucked,” Gilbert explains. “The bus company that built it up, just bad. So it went back. We’re having another bus built right now.”

Brantley Already Knows What Kind of Dad He'll Be

Gilbert’s baby — his first child — will arrive in November, so “The Weekend” singer has some time to get it right. He’s also looked for help from fellow country star Eric Church and his wife Katherine, who consistently travel with their young’ns.

“We talked to Eric and his wife about kind of the set up on their bus and stuff,” Gilbert says. “’Cause down the road we want to make sure that what we’re building right now, that we can convert it to take care of that cause I told [Amber], ‘They’re gonna be with me.’”

Church has traveled with wife and sons, Boone and Hawkins, virtually since the day each was born and has even referenced his backstage area to a play area. Gilbert appears to be next in line. His latest single, “The Ones That Like Me” from his latest album The Devil Don’t Sleep, is set for a summer impact.
