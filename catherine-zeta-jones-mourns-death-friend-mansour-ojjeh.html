Catherine Zeta-Jones mourns death of her 'birthday brother' in heartbreaking tribute

The actress took to Instagram in memory of her friend

    Catherine Zeta-Jones took to Instagram on Sunday in memory of her dear friend, Mansour Ojjeh. The actress shared a series of candid snapshots and a heartfelt message following the news that the Paris-born Saudi billionaire had passed away at the age of 68.

    "Mansour Ojjeh, thank you birthday brother for the joy you gave me in calling you a friend," the heartbroken star wrote.
