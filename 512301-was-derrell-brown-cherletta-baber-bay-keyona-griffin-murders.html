Was Derrell Brown found after Cherletta Baber-Bay and Keyona Griffin murders?

In 2019, a horrific double murder happened in Grand Rapids, Michigan. Cherletta Baber-Bey and Keyona Griffins were found lifeless in their home. After a lengthy investigation, Derrell Brown was marked as a person of interest in the case. But he mysteriously disappeared and was put on the US marshal's most wanted list. So, was Derrell Brown found?

On 13th March 2019, Grand Rapids Police Department, Michigan, received a 911 call from someone who only identified herself as Keyona. She was in distress and needed quick help.

The police arrived at the scene after seven minutes but left having not made contact with anyone. They received a second call two hours later, and this time, they found Cherletta Baber-Bey, 47, and Keyona Griffin, 25, dead in their home on Sheldon Avenue.

Derrell Brown and Cherletta Baber-Bey

Cherletta’s closest family members described her as nurturing, trusting, and sweet but anti-social. "Jay" (Derrell Brown) was her first real boyfriend, and the couple kept to themselves, spending most of their time indoors in the bedroom.

Derrell Brown and Cherletta Baber-Bey met at a downtown Grand Rapids public library. Cherletta routinely visited the library with her mother, but one day when alone, she met Derrell. They fell in love, and he moved into her home.

Cherletta shared no information about Brown with her family members, and most didn't care because he seemed calm and harmless.

The only things that Cherletta's family members knew about "Jay" were that he didn't have a job or phone and wasn't using Facebook. In addition, they knew that Jay had served for failing to pay child support.

Derrell Brown and Keyona Griffin

At the time of their murder Cherletta, 47, lived in Grand Rapids, Michigan, with Derrell, while her lovely niece, Keyona Griffin, 25, visited them occasionally. Keyona had an incredible bond with her aunt and would visit her whenever she had time.

However, Keyona, who had just turned 25 some days before the murder, wasn't fond of her aunt's boyfriend. She found him odd and weird. On 13th March 2019, she made the 911 call but could barely talk. Keyona informed the police that "he" had killed her aunt, and she was next.

Has Derrell Demon Brown been found?

After the murder incident, the police took a lot of time to identify a suspect. Therefore Derrell Demon Brown had enough time to vanish. In the initial investigation, there were zero leads, making it challenging to identify a suspect.

The authorities thoroughly examined the murder scene and its surroundings for a witness but were unsuccessful. They even interviewed some of Cherletta and Keyona's acquaintances, but none had an idea of anyone that could want to harm the two lovely women.

The police decided to look into Cherletta and Keyona's interpersonal relationship only to discover that Cherletta had a boyfriend with a lengthy criminal record, including domestic violence and felony assault.

At first, Derrell Brown was considered a person of interest in the case. But his continued absence raised more suspicion. Furthermore, the murder weapon was retrieved and traced to a woman who had reported it missing in 2017. Surprisingly this woman had dated Derrell Brown before.

Derrell Demon Brown update

There is no latest update about Derrell Brown's whereabouts. US Marshalls think that his relatives might be helping him to evade capture. He has family in Georgia, Pennsylvania, Arizona, and Ohio.

Wrapping up
