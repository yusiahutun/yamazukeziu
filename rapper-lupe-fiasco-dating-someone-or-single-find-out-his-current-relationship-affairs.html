Quick Facts about Lupe Fiasco

 

  • Born on February 16, 1982, in Chicago, Illinois, United States.
  • He is the son of Shirley and Gregory.
  • His father was a drummer and a Karate teacher.
  • His parents divorced when he was five years old.
  • He attended Thornton Township High School.
  • While studying in the sixth grade, he moved with his father full-time.
  • During his high school days, he met Bishop G. and they started creating music.
  • At the age of 18, he started creating his own music in his father's basement.
  • He made his first discography in 2006 titled, Lupe Fiasco's Food & Liquor.
  • He has won 1 Grammy Award and MTV2 Award.
