Hailey Bieber just added a 11,600 Saint Laurent coat to her winter wardrobe

Hailey Bieber is the ultimate Saint Laurent poster girl. 

Don’t believe us? Aside from her frequently donning the French maison’s killer gowns to almost every red carpet event this year this week she has been spotted in the dream winter wardrobe, consisting of three (yes, three) Saint Laurent by Anthony Vacarello coats.

Hailey's faux-fur Saint Laurent coat is dreamy

It appears she saved the best for last, stepping out in New York with her husband Justin Bieber in Saint Laurent’s Mid Length Fur Coat, which is actually available to shop online (if you have £11,600 lying around that is.) For any animal lovers out there you’ll be pleased to know her latest coat is faux, “Saint Laurent's sophisticated retro codes define this long faux-fur coat, shaped with an exaggerated wide lapel collar and streamlined panelled pelts,” they explain.

She didn’t stop there of course. She paired the coat with a Saint Laurent Cowl Neck Dress In Shiny Jersey, their Le 5 À 7 Hobo Bag In Patent Leather and the brand’s Melody Sandal with Calzedonia 15 Denier Resistant Sheer Tights. The look was old money personified and frankly, we are obsessed. Sharing photos of her most recent outfit with her Instagram followers she captioned the series of photos, “I ❤️ NY forever.”

The same day she was photographed in Saint Laurent’s Brown Long Coat In Cashmere Felt, which she wore to grab coffees with her hitmaker hubby. The sartorially savvy couple turned the streets of New York into their own personal catwalk and showed off their contrasting styles. Hailey teamed the outerwear with a black turtleneck, straight leg baggy blue jeans and chunky winter-ready black boots as well as razor thin sunnies. 

She also sported Saint Laurent's Cashmere felt coat

The day before she donned Saint Laurent’s Long Oversize Coat In Chevron Wool, which she wore with her trademark sunglasses, baggy jeans and a Bottega Veneta mini Jodie bag… proving that when it comes to a dream winter wardrobe, Hailey is living out everyone’s fantasy.
