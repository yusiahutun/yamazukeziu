11 best pedicures in London

One of our favourite do-it-all salons is DryBy, the brainchild of Hungarian sisters Krisztina van der Boom and Anita Puluczkai. Inspired by the spa culture in their native Budapest, as well as the nail salons and blow-dry bars in New York, DryBy is the ultimate pitstop for a pampering session.

The pedicures take place downstairs in the chic basement (think modern pink armchairs and white-washed brick walls), with Prosecco on tap. Expect a luxe treatment with a medical edge.

From 21st June to 31st July, DryBy is partnering with aromatic bodycare brand, Mirror Water to offer a pampering pedicure using Mirror Water's soul-soothing bath salts, exfoliator, body oil and balm. You'll leave feeling instantly rejuvenated from the inside out.
