5 Best Tools to Remove Blackheads

It’s bad practice to squeeze blackheads as it can cause damage to your skin, and they’re likely to come back. The best route to treat and remove blackheads is to use gels or a suction device that pull the grit from your pores.

Blackheads are a result of dirt, oils, and grit building up within the pores in your skin. Over time, they become inflamed and more noticeable, especially if you happen to have been born with large pores.

Before you get started

You should open up your pores before you extract your blackheads. You can do this by washing your face with luke-warm water. Once you have extracted, and your face is cleansed, be sure to close your pores by washing your face with cold water.
