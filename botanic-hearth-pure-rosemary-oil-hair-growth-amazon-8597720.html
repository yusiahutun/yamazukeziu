Shoppers Say So Many Hairs Are Sprouting Thanks to This Rosemary Oil

When you consider topical treatments for thinning, shedding hair, perhaps over-the-counter products come to mind. The likes of minoxidil (the key ingredient in products like Rogaine), can certainly make a difference in many cases; that said, pharmaceutical hair growth products may pose potential side effects. Alternatively, there are growth-promoting, follicle-stimulating elixirs, spiked with entirely natural ingredients like caffeine, peppermint, and — perhaps lesser-known — rosemary oil. The lattermost stars in Botanic Hearth’s 100% Pure Rosemary Oil, which shoppers swear sprouts new hair — and improves strength, softness, and shine to boot. The bounties of rosemary — the product’s hero ingredient — extend beyond the culinary realm. In the beauty world, the herb is prized as a natural hair growth-stimulating ingredient. As dermatologist Dr. Marisa Garshick, MD, previously told InStyle, rosemary “is thought to help improve circulation in the scalp,” which, in turn, “may help stimulate hair growth" due to an increase in blood flow. Furthermore, Dr. Garshick shared, studies have shown rosemary may even mimic the growth-promoting prowess of drugs like minoxidil. Bonus benefit: Rosemary oil envelopes strands to add shine and reduce breakage.

In addition to their non-greasy texture, relative to most oils, each offers nutritional benefits for the scalp — which, consequently, creates a healthier environment for hair growth.

The natural nourishers found in Botanic Hearth’s formula creates visible change, according to reviewers.
