Kay Cannon Age, Net Worth, Married, Husband, Children &amp; Wiki

Kay Cannon is an American actress and director, best known for writing and producing the Pitch Perfect film series (2012-2017). She is also known for directing adult comedy film, Blockers. Recently, Cannon starred as Daniella in the television comedy-drama series, Alone Together in 2018.

Cannon is a married woman as of 2019. She is spending romantic and joyful moments of her life with her lovely husband. So, does Cannon share any children with her spouse?

Who Is Kathryn Ellen Cannon? Bio & Wiki

Kay was born as Kathryn Ellen Cannon on 22nd August 1974, in Illinois, United States. She holds an American nationality and belongs to Caucasian white ethnicity. Cannon grew up with her six siblings, Stacie, Matt, Paul, Mary, Luke, and Vince Cannon in Custer Park, Illinois.

Cannon graduated from Reed-Custer High School located in Braidwood, Illinois. Upon graduation, she went to study at Lewis University in Romeoville, Illinois. Later, Cannon received a BA in Theatre and MA in Education degrees from the university.

Kay Cannon’s Career

Kay Cannon started her career from the American action horror mystery film, The Shaft where she acted under the direction of Dick Maas in 2001. Later, she appeared in the short comedy TV show, UCB Comedy Originals in 2007. Cannon also featured in many movies including Mystery Team, The Little Tin Man, and many others.

Who Is Kay Cannon’s Husband?

Kay Cannon is married to Eben Russell, an American screenwriter, and producer. The pair first met on the set of Baby Mama in 2008. Shortly they became friends and started dating each other. Furthermore, after spending a lovely time together, they engaged on Christmas Day in 2010.

The lovebirds tied the knot on 1st January 2012, in Romeoville, Illinois. Likewise, they invited most of their co-actors and family relatives at their wedding. Together, they share a beautiful daughter, Evelyn Rose Russell born on 9th October 2013.

Before Cannon’s marriage with Russell, Cannon was married to Jason Sudeikis on 26th June 2004. Unfortunately, they ended their relationship with divorce on 4th March 2010, citing irreconcilable differences. However, they don’t have any children from their marriage.
