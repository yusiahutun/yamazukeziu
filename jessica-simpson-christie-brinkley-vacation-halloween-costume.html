Jessica Simpson Transforms Into Christie Brinkley for &quot;Vacation&quot; Halloween Costume

Jessica Simpson and hubby Eric Johnson took a trip down "Holiday Road" this Halloween!

The duo dressed up as two characters from "National Lampoon's Vacation" for this year's celebrations -- and totally nailed both of their costumes.

Simpson put her long legs on display as "The woman in the red Ferrari," a role made famous by Christie Brinkley in the 1983 flick. With her hair done up exactly like the supermodel's alluring character, the 35-year-old singer looked just like Brinkley, easily making this one of our favorite costumes this year.

And while Jess was gorgeous, her husband looked eerily similar to Chevy Chase's Clark Griswold, sporting a combover, Member's Only jacket and oversized glasses.

The two even got the seal of approval from Brinkley herself, who took to Instagram last night to chime on their recreation.

"How cute is this? Our National Lampoon was lampooned to PERFECTION," she captioned the pic of their getups. "You guys look Hot and Hilarious!"

We agree.
